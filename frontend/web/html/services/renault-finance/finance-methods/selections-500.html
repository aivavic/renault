    Selections 500

    Renault Finance

    A guaranteed £500 loyalty bonus at the end of every new Renault car Selections agreement*

    Step 1.

    Deposit

    You tell us how much deposit you would like to pay.

    Step 2.

    Balance to fund

    Your salesperson will calculate your monthly payment based on the maximum annual mileage to suit your needs*.

    Step 3.

    Optional final payment

    Your salesperson will calculate the Optional Final Payment depending on your driving requirements.

    WHAT HAPPENS AT THE END OF MY AGREEMENT?

    Three options for when your Renault Selections agreement comes to an end.

    Selection 1

    Change your car for a new Renault

    You can change your car at any Renault dealership. If your car is worth more than the Optional Final Payment, you can use the difference as a deposit for your next new Renault. We’ll also give you an extra £500 towards your deposit* with Selections 500.

    Selection 2

    Give the car back

    You may choose to simply hand the car back to Renault Finance with nothing more to pay (subject to mileage and condition). Excess mileage charge applies. We’ll still give you £500 towards the deposit* for your next new Renault with Selections 500.

    Selection 3

    Keep the car

    You may prefer to keep the car. To do this, simply pay the Optional Final Payment as outlined in your agreement. Renault Finance can help you finance this value, if required.
