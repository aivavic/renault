    R-Link

    The very latest in multimedia features

    Renault R-Link features: A large 7-inch (18-cm) touchscreen which can be programmed to suit your requirements A TomTom navigation system An intuitive voice recognition system which allows you to call contacts or dictate the address you want to drive to An optional parking camera

    • Access the R-Link Store

    TomTom connected navigation

    Discover TomTom connected navigation and the fantastic services it has to offer including LIVE:HD Traffic, which collects the best traffic information in real time and uses it to calculate your estimated driving time and improve your route. LIVE takes into account all the latest traffic bulletins, any road safety warnings, local-area searches and weather forecasts.

    Multimedia made simple

    You can use R-Link to listen to the radio, rock out to your favourite tracks or view your photos and videos (only when stationary), and you can even use it to play children’s games on thanks to Bluetooth, jack and USB connections.

    R-Link applications

    Use the applications which have been pre-installed on the R-Link, including email, Twitter, weather reports and Renault Assistance, or select more from the Renault R-Link catalogue either from the comfort of your car or using your PC.

    Hands-free calling

    When you get into your vehicle, the Renault R-Link synchronizes your phone book and updates your contact list and their photos according to the information available on your phone.

     Making calls is simple: while stationary, you can enter a new number or search for a contact using the R-Link touchscreen and once you are on the move, you can enjoy the convenience of voice recognition

    R-Link connectivity

    Every R-Link version has 7" touch screens with 2d or 3d navigation. Bluetooth connectivity allows you to make calls on the move or play music through another device.

    The R-Link has a voice control feature and an eco function which gives you tips on how to reduce fuel consumption.

    The connectivity of R-link allows you to make driving easier and more entertaining.

You just need to decide where you would like to go!

    Medianav

    a simple complete multimedia system

    Simple and intuitive, with its 7" (18cm) touch screen, MediaNav allows you easy access to a six page menu of features: radio, media, phone, map, navigation and settings.

    Navigation

    Keep it simple

    With MediaNav, you can receive route directions in Navigation mode or just see where you are in Map mode.

    Map mode displays nearby points of interest and favourites and will display the speed limit for the road.

    Navigation mode gives you the route to your destination and can be set by address, point of interest, points on a map or GPS coordinates.

    Enrich your content

    By using the dedicated web interface (https://renault.naviextras.com/shop/portal), you can download updates and purchase new maps which can be installed through the USB port on MediaNav.

    Media

    Play your own music

    With MediaNav, you can play your own music through the car’s speakers by connecting your phone or MP3 player to the USB or Aux socket. The screen will display song and artist information. Connecting to the USB socket will also charge your device.

    Display your stations

    With MediaNav, you can easily find all your favourite radio stations by browsing with the touch screen or via the steering wheel controls.  Stations can be listed alphabetically or by wavelength for ease of use.

    Just fly

    You can change track via the touch screen or the steering wheel controls.  There are normal, shuffle, repeat one and repeat all playback functions.

    Save up to 24 stations

    You can save up to 24 stations (12 FM, 12 AM) as presets in two clicks.

    When listening, MediaNav displays any information broadcast by the radio station such as: song title, artist name and next information coming.

    Phone

    Call hands free

    With the integrated Bluetooth technology, MediaNav allows you to make hands-free calls and syncs your address book and call history.

    Use your phonebook

    Once your phone is paired, contacts are transferred via Bluetooth to the system directory of your MediaNav.  You can then scroll through your contacts or search by name.

    When you receive a call, the Home screen will display all available caller information: phone number and contact name if the number is saved.

    BLUETOOTH TELEPHONE SYSTEM

    Description:

    Renault takes the safety of its passengers as a number one priority. For this reason, Renault has aligned itself with road safety measures to help drivers avoid talking on a hand-held mobile telephone whilst driving.

    The hands-free Bluetooth® system makes the following functions possible without having to handle your phone:

    • Pair up to 5 phones
    • Send / receive / suspend a call
    • Transfer the list of contacts from the phone memory and SIM card (depending on the phone)
    • Access the call logs for calls which have come through the system
    • Edit the vehicle phone memory
    • Dial voicemail
    • Dial emergency numbers

    Your hands-free phone system is only intended to facilitate communication by reducing the risk factors, without removing them completely. You must always comply with the laws of the country in which you are travelling.
