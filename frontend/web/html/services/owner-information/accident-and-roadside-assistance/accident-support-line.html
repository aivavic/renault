    Renault Accident Support Line

    Dedicated support to get you back on the road.

    ACCIDENT SUPPORT LINE

    CALL 0800 085 8005 or 0333 202 3005 *

    Renault Accident Support Line (RASL) is a dedicated accident aftercare service with UK based claim handlers that will deal with your insurance claim in the event of an accident, giving you total support, convenience and peace of mind.

    RASL provides the expertise to assist you through every stage of your claim to get you back on the road with the minimum inconvenience, regardless of fault. My Renault members are automatically covered by RASL, so make sure you register to My Renault to benefit.

    *Calls charged at standard network rate from mobile and landlines.

    Free Roadside Recovery

    24hr Roadside Recovery Service.

    Keeps you mobile

    Like for like replacement vehicle if the accident is not your fault or a courtesy car in other cases.

    Dedicated UK Team

    Our expert claim handlers will assist with all aspects of the UDC TM claim and keep you informed of the progress.

    Only Renault Approved Bodyshops

    Your vehicle will be taken to a Renault Only Approved Accident Repair Centre which deploy factory methods.

    Only Renault genuine parts

    Our Renault Trained Technicians will only fit Genuine Renault Parts, to safeguard your warranty and maintain your vehicles Euro N-Cap status.

    Repair management

    Management of your accident - from Claim to Repair as our processes and procedures are agreed with UK major insurance companies.

    5 year repair guarantee

    We give you a 5 Year Repair Guarantee which will maintain the pre-accident value of the vehicle.

    No excess

    You do not have to pay any excess on your policy if the accident is not your fault.

    Z.E. vehicles Renault Accident Support Line

    Call 0800 169 7985

    A couple of points to remember should you have an accident in your electric vehicle:

    • Care must be taken to ensure that no persons are close to the vehicle after an RTA. The recovery person will determine this at the scene to prevent injury and electric shock
    • A vehicle which has been damaged in an accident can only be recovered on a specialist recovery truck (if undriveable) by a qualified Electric Vehicle operative

    0800 169 7985 is the only number you need in the event of any accident you have, regardless of fault.

    The RASL service is available 24 hours a day - 7 days a week - 365 days a year, covering the UK including Northern Ireland and the Channel Islands.

    How to call for Renault Assistance

    Call 0333 202 3005* or 0800 085 8005

    Before calling Renault Assistance, please make sure that you have the following details ready:

    • Registration number of your vehicle
    • Your name
    • Your address
    • A contact telephone number
    • Model and colour of your vehicle
    • Nature of breakdown
    • Your exact location

    If you believe you are in a vulnerable or dangerous situation, please make this clear at the time of your call. After making your call, return to a safe place near your vehicle. If the problem resolves itself before the assistance arrives, please call and let us know.

    *03 numbers are mobile friendly, charged at national call rates and included in inclusive minute plans from landlines and mobiles. Calls may be monitored and/or recorded.
