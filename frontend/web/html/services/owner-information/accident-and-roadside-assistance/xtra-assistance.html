    Xtra Assistance

    Xtra Assistance is available once your standard Renault Assistance Breakdown Cover has expired. By having a service or MOT carried out at a participating Renault dealer you can purchase one year’s Xtra Assistance for only £30**

    Renault Xtra Assistance includes:

    • Roadside Assistance - Assistance a quarter of a mile or more from your home
    • Home Start Assistance - Assistance at or within a quarter of a mile from your home
    • Local Tow - 10 miles to your closest Renault dealer

    Xtra Assistance standard cover does not include non-warranty incidents, however you can upgrade your Xtra Assistance Cover to include non-warranty incidents for only £80. This upgrade is only available to Xtra Assistance members, and provides cover that could cost well over £200 if bought directly through another recovery programme.

     

    Xtra Assistance Upgrade

    • All Basic Cover items listed above
    • National Recovery
    • One-day car hire
    • Hotel Accommodation
    • Full European Cover
    • The use of incorrect fuel, or lack of fuel
    • Wheel changes, punctured tyres
    • Keys locked inside the vehicle, damaged, lost or stolen
    • Road traffic accidents

    ** Renault Recommended Retail Price. To qualify for Xtra Assistance for £30, your vehicle must have a service or MOT (pre-MOT inspection in Northern Ireland) carried out at a participating Renault Dealer.
    Renault Assistance and Renault Xtra Assistance are provided in partnership with the RAC, from participating Renault Dealers.
