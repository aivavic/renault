    Renault Assistance

    Find out about free Roadside Assistance which comes with any new Renault.

    You’re covered!

    The Renault 4+ package includes 4 years Roadside Assistance*, provided in partnership with the RAC.  Renault Assistance is a vehicle-based membership, so it covers anyone driving your Renault with your permission.



    * for vehicles registered from 1st February 2012

    Click here for a copy of the Renault Assistance Terms and Conditions

     

    How to call for Renault Assistance

    Call 0333 202 3005* or 0800 085 8005

    Before calling Renault Assistance, please make sure that you have the following details ready:

    • Registration number of your vehicle
    • Your name
    • Your address
    • A contact telephone number
    • Model and colour of your vehicle
    • Nature of breakdown
    • Your exact location

    If you believe you are in a vulnerable or dangerous situation, please make this clear at the time of your call. After making your call, return to a safe place near your vehicle. If the problem resolves itself before the assistance arrives, please call and let us know.

    *03 numbers are mobile friendly, charged at national call rates and included in inclusive minute plans from landlines and mobiles. Calls may be monitored and/or recorded.

    Repairing your Renault

    Renault Approved Accident Repair Centres ensure your car and warranty are fully protected.

    You have a choice

    In the unfortunate event of an accident, you have the right to choose where and how your Renault is repaired. Your insurance company may try to convince you that you should have it repaired at their Bodyshop and they may not use genuine Renault parts.

    "Know Your Rights" Our guide will assist you making the right choice as the most important thing in a Renault is you…

    • Know Your Rights Guide

    Why use a Renault Approved Accident Repair Centre?

    Renault Approved Accident Repair Centres meet Renault’s rigorous standards for service and quality, and they can ensure your car and warranty are fully protected to the level of quality that you purchased it at and therefore ensure that you keep your Renault 100% Renault.

    WE GIVE YOU A 5 YEAR REPAIR GUARANTEE

    The Renault Approved Accident Repair Centres will issue a 5 Year Repair Guarantee which will maintain the pre-accident value of your vehicle.

    YOUR 12 YEAR ANTI-CORROSION WARRANTY IS MAINTAINED

    Each of our carefully selected Renault Approved Accident Repair Centres has been chosen to meet the strictest criteria to carry out the best repairs, thus ensuring that the 12-year anti-corrosion warranty on your vehicle is maintained.

    YOUR RENAULT RETAINS ITS EURO NCAP SAFETY RATING

    Renault continuously invests millions of pounds making sure that all vehicles in our range will protect you in the event of an accident making us one of the most successful manufacturers in terms of safety. This has been recognised by the independent and industry wide Euro NCAP crash tests.

    THE LATEST REPAIR TECHNOLOGY IS USED

    As technology changes dramatically, Renault invests heavily in training programmes for our approved body repairers to meet the high standards required to maintain Renault vehicles.

    ONLY RENAULT GENUINE PARTS ARE USED TO SAFEGUARD YOUR WARRANTY

    Renault Approved Accident Repair Centres have full access to genuine Renault Parts which preserve your car’s original driving pleasure, technology, quality, environment and above all, safety. Their use will ensure your vehicle's Euro NCAP rating is maintained whereas the use of non-genuine parts could compromise your safety and that of your passengers.

    Remember some insurance companies may try to convince you that you should have it repaired at their Bodyshop and may not use genuine Renault parts. Before your car is repaired, ask these three questions...

    • Will you be using Renault Genuine Parts on my vehicle – if not, why not?
    • Can I have Renault Genuine Parts used on my vehicle?
    • Could non-genuine parts invalidate my warranty and Euro NCAP safety rating?

      Remember – The Choice Is Yours!
