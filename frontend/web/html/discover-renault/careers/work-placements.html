    Work Placements

    At Renault UK, we are passionate about what we do and we love to share our experience and expertise with talented, interested undergraduates.

    Each year Renault UK provide students with the opportunity to become part of our team for a 12 month business placement within their chosen discipline.  We understand the importance of gaining the right work experience during your studies. Whether you are working in marketing completing essential competitor analysis or in operations measuring the performance of our sales teams and suppliers; we want you to take on responsibility and share your ideas.

    We look for students who want to get involved, who are prepared to take on responsibility and who thrive on learning and meeting new challenges. In return we promise to fully immerse you within our business, support your development and for those who shine, there is potential to progress to our graduate programme.

    Entrant Requirements:

     

    •Studying a degree with placement year option or an approved gap year

    •Have 300 UCAS Points

    •Minimum Grade B in GCSE Maths and English or equivalent.
