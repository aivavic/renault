    Features

    CHARACTERISTICS

    Built in France on the same lines as the Kangoo, Kangoo Z.E. is your partner tailored to your professional needs. Fully modular, it provides a loading area of up to 4.6 m³ for a payload of 650 kg!

    • Modular layout
    • Range
    • Comfort
    • R-LINK

    Business features

    Modular layout

    A choice of bulkheads

    To meet your needs, Kangoo Z.E. ships with a wide variety of bulkheads: tubular, full steel, fully glazed, swivelling mesh bulkhead or fixed mesh bulkhead on the 5-seater Maxi version.

    Folding passenger seat

    With the passenger seat folded, the mesh bulkhead can be swivelled for loading long objects.

    Sliding rear roof flap

    Measuring 47cm by 1m, the rear roof flap lets you load long, bulky objects vertically.

    Crew Cab version

    With the Crew Cab version, you can carry a team of five people with load space of 2.4m³. To carry long or bulky objects, fold the rear seat. Load space increases to 3.6m³ in a single movement! Further, the Crew Cab version is taxed as an LCV, so VAT can be reclaimed under certain conditions.

    Ply Lining

    Protect the interior of your Kangoo with our hardwearing, bespoke ply lining. The price includes fitting by Renault. Our lining kits are fitted without any alteration/drilling to the vehicle structure, ensuring that the paint and anti corrosion warranties remain intact.

    Sliding side door

    Highly convenient for reaching the back of the loading area and for loading/unloading objects!

    Storage areas

    A wide range of storage compartments are available to make your day-to-day work easier! An easily accessible space on the dashboard can hold A4 format note books and small objects. The 15l glovebox is big enough to hold a laptop. The overhead rack provides a wide, deep area above the windscreen. The central armrest is large enough to hold 1.5l bottles.

    Z.E equipment

    Range

    Econometer

    Located on the dashboard behind the wheel, the econometer indicates the remaining range and shows you in real time whether the vehicle is consuming or recovering energy.

    Energy recovery

    To optimize the range of your Kangoo Z.E, the battery recharges during deceleration. That means as soon as you take your foot off the accelerator!

    Pre-heating

    This ingenious system lets you remotely set the heating or ventilation of your Kangoo Z.E. to come on when the vehicle is charged. This ensures a comfortable cabin temperature when you start your day with no impact on vehicle range!

    Eco mode

    Kangoo Z.E. ships as standard with Eco mode. This lets you extend your range by up to 10% by reducing motor performance (torque and power). For improved ergonomics, the position of the button has been changed. It is now located between the two front seats.

    Z.E. Voice

    Z.E. The Voice function emits a sound to warn pedestrians that your car is coming. It is active at speeds of between 0.6 and 18.6mph. This function gives you peace of mind when driving in the city!

    Additional heating

    The optional additional heating function on Kangoo Z.E. runs on diesel or biodiesel (B30). Replacing the electric heating function, it ensures efficient thermal comfort with no trade-off in range. In cold weather, you gain several dozen miles!

    Low-consumption tyres

    Kangoo Z.E. is equipped with Michelin Energy Saver tyres. Take advantage of their low rolling resistance, with no impact on braking or roadholding.

    Connected services

    With the My Z.E. Pack Log on and view charging information for your Kangoo Z.E. from your computer or smartphone. My Z.E. Inter@ctive lets you activate remote charging of your vehicle if it is connected to a station. A good way to optimize your budget by scheduling charging for offpeak periods!

    COMFORT & CONVENIENCE

    Comfort

    Bluetooth and USB radios

    All the radios in the Kangoo Z.E. range feature a USB socket and Bluetooth® function. So you can make calls while keeping your hands on the wheel*!

    * To make phone calls safely, we recommend that you take a break.

    Cruise control-speed limiter

    Select a constant speed (cruise control function) or set a maximum speed (speed limiter).

    Rear parking sensor

    During manoeuvres, the function warns you of any obstacles with a series of beeps that become more frequent as you move closer. A function that will prove to be extremely useful in the city!

    Rain and light sensor

    Located on the windscreen behind the interior rearview mirror, the sensor switches the lights on automatically when it gets dark and activates the wipers if it starts to rain.

    Voice command

    Renault R-LINK is equipped with an innovative voice command function that lets you call a contact or dictate the address of your destination. A great way to save time at the wheel of your Kangoo Z.E!

    Steering column controls.

    Adjust the radio easily without releasing the wheel.

    MULTIMEDIA

    R-Link

    Rediscover multimedia through simple features tailored to vehicle use, including a large touchscreen, modern interfaces and Bluetooth®, along with USB sockets and a jack on the front panel. Listening to music from a portable device or viewing photos has never been easier! Also explore Renault R-Link Store, the first automotive app store! Download a wide variety of apps from your computer via MyRenault, or directly from your vehicle.

    TELEPHONE FUNCTION

    Making calls is easy! When the car is stationary, simply enter a number or browse your contacts on the Renault R-Link touchscreen. What's more, when you get into your vehicle, Renault R-Link automatically synchronises your directory and displays your contact list along with photos (depending on the telephone).

    NAVIGATION

    Take advantage of the TomTom® connected navigation system and LIVE services such as HD Traffic® - the best in real-time traffic information systems. R-Link helps you manage the range of your Kangoo Z.E. by displaying charging stations on the map. It also lets you see how far the vehicle can go, based on range, and whether it has the capacity to reach your planned destination.

    SERVICES AND VEHICLES

    Make life easier with the pre-installed R-Link apps: Email, Renault R-Link Tweet, Weather and Renault Assistance. Access the R-Link Store catalogue and discover a range of apps featuring car data (e-guide, fuel prices, etc.), news, practical information (TomTom places, etc.) or relating to leisure, culture or tourism!
