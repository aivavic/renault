    MEDIA GALLERY

    Grand Scenic Limited

    LIMITED EDITION

    Do you want nothing but the best? For those who appreciate the finer things in life, we have created a Limited range. Satisfy your every desire and stand out with a unique look!

    Magnetic design

    Make your mark with Grand Scénic Limited's 16" wheel rims, LED daytime running lights and tinted windows.

    Loaded with equipment as standard

    Don't compromise. Experience top-of-the-range comfort with the leather steering wheel as standard, dual-zone climate control, electronic parking brake and reversing camera.

    Engines

    Grand Scénic Limited features high-performance, economical petrol and diesel engines. To meet all of your needs, choose between a six-speed manual gearbox or an automatic dual-clutch gearbox.

    Grand Scenic Bose®+ pack

    BOSE®+ pack

    Renault has teamed up with Bose® to offer you a unique audio experience. Custom-designed for your Renault, the Bose® sound system delivers a superb natural, sharp sound.

    Exterior design

    17" black diamond-cut aluminium wheel rims and the Bose® badge on the front wings: Grand Scénic Bose®+ Pack asserts its sporty elegance.

    What is Bose®?

    The Bose® Energy Efficient Series sound system has 8 speakers, an integrated subwoofer and a digital amplifier to give you authentic sound on-board.

    Equipment and interior design

    Climb inside a refined interior, in perfect harmony with the Bose® philosophy. The spacious interior is finished with glossy black trim, with a steering wheel and gear stick that really set the tone!
