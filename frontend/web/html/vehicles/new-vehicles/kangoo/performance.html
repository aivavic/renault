    Performance

    PERFORMANCE

    We have equipped the Kangoo with new, economical, high-performance engines. The ENERGY engines make it the segment leader in terms of fuel consumption and offer you greater efficiency, ensuring better-regulated fuel consumption.

    FOR MORE INFORMATION ON

    • DCI 75 and 90 ENGINES
    • ENERGY DCI 110 engine
    • ENERGY TCE 115 ENGINE
    • Regulated fuel consumption

    DCI 75 and 90 ENGINES

    The Energy dCi 75 and Energy dCi 90* engines offer optimum energy performance thanks to technology such as Stop & Start, combined with an energy recovery system on braking/deceleration. This system automatically places the engine on standby when the vehicle is stationary, cutting out all pollutant emissions. When the driver puts the car in gear again, it restarts almost instantly. The starter has been reinforced to allow it to cope with multiple restarts.

    The lifecycle cost of the Kangoo has been greatly reduced, with fuel consumption of just 4.3L/100 km* and emissions of 112g of CO2/km*.

    ENERGY DCI 110 ENGINE

    Fuel efficiency and performance

    This engine is characterised by 110 horsepower at 4,000rpm, suitable for large loads and long journeys. Still with practicality in mind, the torque of 240Nm is available from 1,750 rpm. As 80% of this total torque is available from 1,500rpm, the Energy dCi 110 is especially suited to starting with a full load, as well as pick-up and acceleration in all circumstances. This engine combines technical innovations that improve the performance of the turbo, without increasing fuel consumption. In addition, the Stop & Start cuts out all pollutant emissions when stationary. All of these features allow the Kangoo to boast fuel consumption of 4.4 L/100 km* and CO2 emissions of just 115 g/km*.

    ENERGY TCE 115 ENGINE

    The alternative to diesel

    Efficient in terms of power, torque and fuel consumption, the Energy TCe 115 is especially suited to your professional needs, whilst offering excellent pick up and acceleration. It is a 1.2 litre (1,198 cm3) turbo, 4-cylinder, 16-valve, direct-injection engine. This engine, which boasts 115 horsepower, offers you an excellent maximum torque (torque = for starting, pick up and acceleration) of 190Nm available from 2,000 to 4,000 rpm - 90% of which kicks in at 1,500rpm. On top of this performance, the Tce 115 is also fuel-efficient, with consumption of just 5.9 L/100 km* and CO2 emissions of 140g/km* thanks to a combination of new technologies.

    Regulated fuel consumption

    Segment leader in terms of fuel consumption

    Thanks to a collection of technologies such as Stop & Start, energy recovery on braking, low-pressure EGR and thermomanagement, the Kangoo is equipped with engines focused on improving energy performance. With these new Energy dCi 75 and Energy dCi 90 engines, the Kangoo comes as standard with gear change indicators and Eco Mode, which reduces fuel consumption by up to 10% depending on the driving conditions and driving style*.

    *Official Fuel consumption figures are produced for comparison purpose and are measured according to the European Directive 80/1268/EEC. These figures are specific for a vehicle type and not for a single car. Real world fuel (actual) consumption can vary as the fuel efficiency of a vehicle is influenced by driving behaviour, weather and road conditions. The use of technical equipment for example air conditioning, can also effect the fuel consumption. For further information, please visit the Vehicle Certification Agency web site www.vca.gov.uk.
