    PRACTICAL & CLEVER

    Comfort & Convenience

    Renault Clio shows once again that “size matters”. Here again, we are making a difference through our ability to develop a simple offering with features that are original or usually reserved for high-end vehicles. Expressing comfort in every detail, Renault Clio creates a modern, welcoming and ergonomic interior for passengers.

    BOOT SPACE

    Renault Clio features a 300dm3 (VDA standard) boot, on a par with the best in its class. Roomy!

    Glovebox

    A large 7l glove compartment, able to hold a 1.5l water bottle.

    Central armrest

    The central armrest optimises driving comfort and allows you to store small items such as your wallet. Perfect for keeping the essentials within easy reach.

    Door compartments

    Two compartments in the front and rear, each with a capacity of 1.5l. Plenty of space to stow your maps or a bottle.

    Cup holder

    Smart storage to safely store bottles and cups.

    ALL THE COMFORT OF A BIG CAR

    Comfort & Convenience

    AIR CONDITIONING

    The air conditioning system with automatic recirculation via a toxicity sensor lets passengers at the front select their ideal temperature, to the exact degree (only available with R-Link).

    Reclining, foldable rear seating

    Clio comes with a 60:40 split/fold rear seating at all levels of the range. Long or bulky items can be carried easily from time to time!

    Rain and light sensor

    The automatic speed control windscreen wipers start and adapt their rate to the intensity of the rain. What's more, a light sensor automatically switches on the headlamps when night falls or when you enter a tunnel (depending on equipment level).

    Heated wing mirrors

    A resistor behind the mirror heats the surface when the function is activated.

    Toxicity sensor

    Built into the air conditioning system, a toxicity sensor and a combined pollen/activated carbon filter help reduce the level of fine particles (such as pollen and dust) and pollution inside the passenger compartment. Available as an option only with R-Link.

    MULTIMEDIA FOR ALL

    Technology

    Innovation rules! Renault Clio comes with two new onboard multimedia systems: MEDIA NAV and Renault R-Link* (available as standard or optional depending on the trim level). Choose the Renault R-Link digital tablet for easy access to innovative and practical functions, including TomTom® LIVE navigation (available depending on the country), multimedia, telephone and vehicle information. Digital technology and cars have never worked so well together!

    Hands-free card

    Just carry the card with you! Unlock, lock and start the car without inserting the card into the reader (depending on equipment level).

    Renault R-Link Multimedia System

    Enjoy fast access to innovative and practical functions: TomTom connected navigation, multimedia, hands-free telephone and vehicle information. Also explore the R-Link Store, the complete catalogue of apps downloadable from R-Link (available as an option).

    HILL-START ASSIST

    Highly useful when starting the vehicle on steep slopes, facing uphill or downhill. The function holds the vehicle for two seconds, giving you time to move off calmly.

    Rear parking sensor

    This function warns you of any obstacles behind the car via a series of beeps that increase in frequency as you move closer.

    Reversing camera

    Parallel parking? Nothing could be easier with the built-in reversing camera at the rear of the vehicle, with the picture on the R-Link screen.

    RENAULT BASS REFLEX®

    Discover the original BASS REFLEX system, able to recreate the volume and listening pleasure of a 30l subwoofer. Enjoy improved low-frequency playback quality as well as a clearer and more audible sound.

    MEDIANAV Multimedia System

    Discover the 100% useful, simple and smart multimedia system available with Clio! See for yourself, with its large 7" touchscreen, steering-column controls, standard navigation system, 4 * 20W radio, Bluetooth®, USB sockets and jack (depending on equipment level).

    R-Plug & Radio

    Radio with built-in display, Bluetooth® (hands-free telephone and music audio-streaming), USB socket/jack and steering-column controls.

    3D SOUND BY ARKAMYS® with R-Link

    Using digital signal processing software, this system adds depth and relief to the sound by recreating a spatial effect.You can also select where you want the sound in the passenger compartment: driver’s seat only, front seats only, or the whole vehicle (only with R-Link).

    Cruise control - speed limiter

    Set your cruising speed or maximum speed using the cruise-control-speed limiter.

    MULTIMEDIA

    R-Link

    Rediscover multimedia through simple features tailored to vehicle use, including a large touchscreen, modern interfaces and Bluetooth®, along with USB sockets and a jack on the front panel. It’s never been easier to listen to your playlists and view photos and videos while the car is stationary! Also explore the Renault R-Link Store, the first in-car App Store! Download a wide variety of applications from your computer via MyRenault, or directly from your vehicle.

    Telephone

    Making calls is easy: when the car is stationary, simply enter a number or browse your contacts on the Renault R-Link touchscreen. When driving, keep your hands on the wheel and your eyes on the road, using the voice recognition system! What's more, when you get into your vehicle, Renault R-Link synchronises your directory and displays your contact list along with photos (depending on the telephone).

    NAVIGATION

    Take advantage of the TomTom® connected navigation system and LIVE services such as HD Traffic® - the best in real-time traffic information systems! Make travelling easier with the latest traffic information and maps of Europe (available as an option).

    SERVICES AND VEHICLES

    Make life easier with the pre-installed R-Link apps: Email, Renault R-Link Tweet, Weather and Renault Assistance. Access the complete catalogue of Renault R-Link apps from your computer via MyRenault, or directly from Clio. Would you like to hear the sound of a V8 engine whenever you accelerate? Select R-Sound Effect to simulate a different engine noise, just for fun!

    RELAXED DRIVING

    Safety

    Top-level passive safety

    Clio was awarded 5 stars in the EuroNcap passive safety crash tests.

    Isofix system

    The rear side seats are equipped with the ISOFIX child seat safety system. Your child seats will be attached securely and safely.

    Emergency brake assist

    Standard, along with the Anti-lock Brake System (ABS). Emergency brake assist reduces stopping distances by applying full braking power more quickly.

    Electronic Stability Control (ESC)

    ESC helps you keep the vehicle under control in the event of a temporary loss of grip. Braking applied transparently to one or more wheels keeps the vehicle on course.

    Anti-lock Braking System (ABS)

    The ABS senses when a wheel is about to lock up. It then reduces the braking pressure on that wheel so that it can regain traction. Normal braking pressure will resume as soon as possible.
