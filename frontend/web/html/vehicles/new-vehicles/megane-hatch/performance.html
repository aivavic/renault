    Packed with technology

    Looking for more technology, more driving pleasure and more savings? The Energy engines will surprise you with their smooth response. Downsizing (reduced capacity), Stop & Start: engines packed with innovations! More than ever, Mégane is placing the emphasis on reliability: reliability in everyday use, reliability for your budget, reliability in your affections.

    ENERGY ENGINES AND EDC AUTOMATIC GEARBOX

    Get a turbocharged boost with the TCe 130 engine mated to the EDC automatic gearbox. The know-how of our Formula 1® engineers at your service, delivering a smooth response at all revs. With torque of 205Nm at 2,000 rpm, it packs a real punch! Along with lower fuel consumption: 49.55/mpg* and CO2 emissions of just 130g of CO2/km*. The EDC gearbox delivers smooth, automatic gear changes. You can switch back to non-automatic mode at any time.


    ENERGY TCe 115 / ENERGY TCe 130 with overboost

    Petrol engines

    User savings and respect for the environment: The Mégane Energy TCe 115 consumes just 53.2mpg* for CO2 emissions of 119g / km* over a combined cycle (52.3 mpg* and CO2 emissions of 124g / km* with the Energy TCe 130).

    The Energy TCe 115/130 engine is a 1.2l (1,198 cm3) turbocharged, direct-injection unit, with four cylinders and 16 valves. Capacity reduced by 25% with no trade-off in performance or fuel efficiency. The Energy TCe 115 develops 115hp for maximum torque (torque = pick-up and acceleration) of 190Nm, available between 2,000 and 4,000rpm.

    Energy dCi 110

    Diesel engine

    Even more savings with Stop&Start technology: it saves fuel when the vehicle is stationary and starts it again almost immediately, as part of a smooth, vibration-free process

    Mégane consumes just 80mpg* (over a combined cycle) of CO2 emissions of only 90g/km*! Developing 110hp at 4,000rpm, the Energy dCi 110 delivers high torque of 260Nm from 1,750 rpm. An economical drive.

    Energy dCi 130

    Diesel engine

    Take advantage of a powerful, dynamic high-performance 130hp diesel engine for your Mégane. With consumption of 70 mpg* for CO2 emissions of 104g /km*. The highly efficient turbocharger delivers generous torque (320 Nm) available at low revs and across a broad band. This quietly discreet engine offers smooth pick-up and acceleration for a relaxing drive.


    *Official Fuel consumption figures are produced for comparison purpose and are measured according to the European Directive 80/1268/EEC. These figures are specific for a vehicle type and not for a single car. Real world fuel (actual) consumption can vary as the fuel efficiency of a vehicle is influenced by driving behaviour, weather and road conditions. The use of technical equipment for example air conditioning, can also effect the fuel consumption. For further information, please visit the Vehicle Certification Agency web site www.vca.gov.uk.
