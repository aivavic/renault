    Features

    Innovative and comfortable

    CHARACTERISTICS

    Mégane is packed with features and equipment! Dual-zone air conditioning, heated seats and a rain and light sensor, all contribute to high standards in driving comfort.

    • Comfort
    • multimedia
    • Technology
    • Safety

    Driving comfort

    Comfort

    Discover all the comfort features on Mégane Hatch! A wide range of practical onboard functions and innovations for real driving pleasure.

    DUAL-ZONE AIR CONDITIONING

    The temperature can be adjusted for each front seat passenger.

    Auto mode ensures balanced temperature management, while Fast or Soft modes place the emphasis on temperature or acoustics.

    SUNROOF

    The electric sunroof gives you a panoramic view of the exterior while letting the light flood in.

    Heated front seats

    Enjoy high standards of comfort. The front seats are heated and position adjusted with lumbar and height controls.

    LIGHT AND RAIN SENSOR

    The automatic speed-sensitive windscreen wipers switch on automatically and adapt their speed to the intensity of the rain.
    A light sensor automatically switches on the headlights when night falls or when you enter a tunnel.

    Digital instrument cluster

    From your comfortable seat, access key data: speedometer with a digital readout, cruise-control / speed limiter indicator.

    Wing mirror

    Electric, demisting and electric folding.

    'Clear view' function

    This function makes life easier in winter by quickly demisting and de-icing the windows.

    Electronic parking brake

    When the engine is turned off, the system applies the brake automatically.

    BOOT

    Maximum storage capacity of 368 litres.

    Stay connected

    Multimedia

    Gain easy access to the functions of your Mégane, designed for simplicity and convenience. Take a call, listen to music from a portable device or view photos and videos when the car is stationary.

    Renault R-Link

    Innovative functions with touch or voice control! Multimedia, navigation, TomTom® and telephone. The choice is up to you.

    Telephone

    Make phone calls or send emails by voice command. You can also safely hear the RSS feed and text from some apps using the Text to Speech automatic reading function.

    R-LINK STORE

    Use the pre-installed apps: Email, Renault R-Link Tweet, Weather, Renault Assistance. Access the complete catalogue of Renault R-Link apps from your Mégane by registering with My Renault.

    Take full advantage of your journey

    Technology

    The latest innovative technologies from Renault make everyday life simpler: a reversing camera for easier manoeuvres, dual-zone air conditioning, and more.

    EDC automatic gearbox

    Control upward and downward gear changes for exceptional drive at all times.

    The new EDC automatic gearbox is as responsive as a manual gearbox in all circumstances:

    • instant response to driver input, in both automatic and flick-shift mode.
    • flick-shift mode lets the driver override the automatic system.

    Silent timing chain*

    This lifetime timing chain is maintenance-free. It also significantly improves engine noise and increases durability.

    * With the Energy TCe 115 and 130 and the Energy dCi 130 engines.

    Hands-free Renault card

    With the hands-free card, no more fumbling for the door lock on your Mégane. The car can be unlocked, locked and started without manual operation input.

    REAR PARKING DISTANCE CONTROL

    Designed to make parking manoeuvres easier! The parking distance control function warns you of obstacles in front of or behind the car.

    Equipped for your safety

    Safety

    Make the road even safer with Visio System®. Using a camera at the front, this technology identifies the road markings and position of the car. In this way, Mégane Hatch warns you if you drift out of your lane and also turns on the high-beam lights when road conditions require.

    ELECTRONIC STABILITY CONTROL (ESC)

    The ASR traction control and under-steer correction function increase vehicle stability*. This is a real advantage in difficult driving conditions.

    * Depending on the version.

    VISIO SYSTEM®

    Using a high-resolution camera, this system warns you if the car crosses a broken or unbroken line. It also switches from high-beam to low-beam in the city.

    SEAT BELT

    To reduce force in the event of impact and keep the driver and passengers in their seats, the belts are fitted with pretensioners at the front and load limiters at the rear.

    ISOFIX SYSTEM

    The rear side seats are equipped with an ISOFIX anchor points child seat securing system, to safely and securely fit a child seat.

    ABS ANTI-LOCK BRAKES WITH EBV ELECTRONIC BRAKE DISTRIBUTION

    Along with the emergency braking system, this function helps you to control your car and keep it on course.

    Cruise control - speed limiter

    Use the steering wheel controls to adjust and limit your speed. In limiter mode, a signal warns you if you exceed the set speed.
