    Battery & Charging

    Battery & Charging

    Just like the other models in the Renault Z.E. Range, Twizy is fitted with a lithium-ion battery, offering great performance. It is very simple to charge, even when it is fully discharged. It can also be recycled at the end of its life.

    Z.E. Battery

    Hiring a battery keeps monthly costs affordable.

    The battery hire agreement guarantees you a minimum charge capacity* and full recovery services in the event of all types of breakdown.

    You can change the mileage and terms of the agreement at any time until 3 months before the end of the hire term. Any new monthly payment will reflect the prices shown. If the revised payment is greater than the original payment you'll be charged for any additional costs up to the date of the change to the terms. Excess mileage is charged at £11.25 (incl. VAT) per 100 miles. In the event that the agreement is terminated additional charges may apply.

    * Charge capacity of 75% of the original capacity at delivery.

    British Gas

    The optimum partner in charging Z.E. vehicles.

    British Gas are experts in the field of electric vehicle charging solutions. As Renault's nationwide charging partner they can provide more convenient, effective and safer charging. They have a range of solutions to make charging work well for your home or for your business to ensure that your electric vehicle/s are always ready to run.

    So, whether you're thinking of buying an electric vehicle for personal use, company use or as a sole trader, British Gas have got the charging answer for you.

    What's more an Off-Peak Saver tariff^ is available to enable you to charge your car when electricity on this tariff is at its cheapest complemented by the option of spreading the cost of a dedicated home charging solution over 12 months with our British Gas Payment Plan†.
