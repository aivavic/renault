    Comfort for all

    Comfort

    AUTOMATIC AIR CONDITIONING

    Offered as standard, the air conditioning system with combined filter lets you select the exact temperature you desire. The Eco Mode function even lets you optimise your driving range!

    TAKE CARE BY RENAULT

    Offered as standard on the ZOE Dynamique Zen version, the 'Take Care by Renault' pack is entirely devoted to your well-being. It includes an ioniser, a toxicity sensor and active fragrances.

    3D SOUND BY ARKAMYS

    This system gives the sound depth and texture by creating a spatial effect. You can also select where you want the sound in the passenger compartment: driver’s seat only, front seats only, or the whole vehicle (only with R-Link).

    REAR PARKING SENSORS WITH REVERSING CAMERA

    Thanks to the parking distance control system, you no longer need to worry about obstacles behind you! This function warns you via a series of beeps that increase in frequency as you move closer. With the reversing camera, the R-Link screen shows you the space available to park and the trajectory according to the angle of the steering wheel.

    HANDS-FREE CARD

    Just carry the card with you! Unlock, lock and start the car without inserting the card into the reader.

    RAIN AND LIGHT SENSORS

    The automatic speed control windscreen wipers start and adapt their rate to the intensity of the rain. A light sensor automatically switches on the headlamps when night falls or when you enter a tunnel (depending on equipment level).

    DEMISTING DOOR MIRRORS

    A resistor behind the mirror heats the surface when the function is activated.

    HILL-START ASSIST

    This function comes in extremely useful when starting your vehicle on steep slopes, facing uphill or downhill. ZOE is held in position for 2 seconds, giving you time to move off calmly.

    Innovation at your service

    Technology

    Your ZOE contains the most advanced electric technology: more than 60 patents were filed over the course of its design! Innovations designed to enhance driving range, ease of use and connectivity. Let's take a closer look at these cutting-edge features. They have allowed ZOE to become the first mass-produced electric vehicle to be certified with a driving range of 121 miles** (NEDC cycle*).

    Let's look at the 'Range Optimizer' system, which improves driving range regardless of the driving conditions. As with a fuel-powered vehicle, your speed and driving style, the condition of the roads taken and the use of heating or air conditioning all have an impact on the actual driving range. This means that, on a suburban route, you can achieve an actual driving range of approximately 71 miles during the winter season and around 105 miles at warmer times of the year

    *NEDC: New European Driving Cycle, the European standard for emissions and consumption, is a method for measuring fuel consumption based on identical tests carried out on all types of vehicles in Europe, whether they are internal combustion, electric, or hybrid. It is, therefore, an objective test for comparing performance between models of various manufacturers. There are two parts to the test. The vehicle is put on a roller bench and driven through the same urban cycle (cycle ECE-15) three times, followed by a single extra-urban cycle. The average of these four cycles gives the average NEDC range. Please see the technical specifications page for more details on range. **The official NEDC driving range is 121 miles with 17" wheels.

    R-Link

    Technology

    Rediscover multimedia through simple features tailored to vehicle use, including a large touchscreen, modern interfaces and Bluetooth®, along with USB sockets and a jack on the front panel. It’s never been easier to listen to your playlists and view photos and videos while the car is stationary!

    TELEPHONE

    Making calls couldn't be easier! When stationary, you can enter a number or browse your contacts on the Renault R-Link touchscreen. When driving, keep your hands on the wheel and your eyes on the road, using the voice recognition system. What's more, when you get into your vehicle, Renault R-Link automatically synchronises your directory and displays your contact list along with photos (depending on the telephone).

    NAVIGATION

    Your ZOE is equipped with Carminat TomTom Z.E. LIVE as standard. This technology offers you the most energy-efficient route and locates charge points on the map. It displays the activity radius around your vehicle based on its driving range. Choose a destination and the system will tell you if you can reach it.

    SERVICES AND VEHICLES

    Make life easier with the pre-installed R-Link apps: Email, Renault R-Link Tweet, Weather and Renault Assistance. Access the complete catalogue of Renault R-Link apps from your computer via MyRenault, or directly from your ZOE. Would you like to hear the sound of a V8 engine whenever you accelerate? Select R-Sound Effect to simulate a different engine noise, just for fun!

    RELAXED DRIVING

    Safety

    The ZOE was designed from the start to be fully electric. As with all Renault cars, your safety was made the top priority during its development.

    A 5-STAR ZOE

    Not only was the ZOE awarded five stars in the passive safety tests conducted by the independent body Euro NCAP, it was also elected 'best supermini of 2013', all models included! The new testing barometer has 4 criteria: protection for adults, children and pedestrians and driving assistance. With an overall score of 82/100, the ZOE is among the leaders in its segment!

    BATTERY SAFETY

    Special integration work went into the battery pack located in the floor, in order to preserve its integrity. Due to this, it is fully protected against impacts and perforations.

    SILENCE AND PEDESTRIANS

    Your ZOE is silent, but don't worry: it warns pedestrians of its approach with Z.E. Voice! Choose between three different sounds to make your ZOE heard when driving at low speeds. If necessary, you can also disconnect Z.E. Voice.
