    British Gas

    Z.E. Charging Partner

    The optimum partner in charging Z.E. vehicles.

    Wall box included customers

    No fuss, clean, easy - these are all things that we love about Renault ZOE.

    Exclusively for retail customers, a combination of government funding and Renault funding means our charging partner, British Gas, can install a free 7KW tethered wall box at your home when you order a ZOE.

    Its 7kW capacity means that your ZOE’s battery can be recharged from completely flat to 100% in just 3 to 4 hours, and Z.E. Ready certification means it’ll work perfectly with the car. 

    As long as you have a domestic property with off-street parking and an O2 data signal, you’re eligible – British Gas will check all this for you. 

    The process is simple , once your ZOE is ordered, British Gas will contact you automatically and schedule your installation at your convenience. The entire process normally takes 2-3 weeks once your ZOE has been ordered.

    Charging

    No fuss, clean and easy, for your everyday life.

    Charging Network

    A charging network is a network of publicly accessible charge points of various amounts of power. This system has been created through the combination of work by network providers, car manufacturers and the government.

    In total there are over 6500 charge points spread across the UK that are accessible to the public through the use of R.F.I.D cards and smart phone applications.

    The main manufacturers of these are:

    Ecotricity

    Ecotricity has its own charging network called ‘the Ecotricity Electric Highway’. This is a growing network of over 500 rapid chargers that are free to use. The points are located along motorways and aim to connect EV drivers across the UK.

    • Register for your free ecotricity card

    Pod Point

    The Pod Point network is one of the UK’s earliest EV charging networks. A £12.50 annual registration will give you access to about 1200 charging points spread over the UK. It works with a number of different providers to offer a range of charging options from 3kW to 43kW. Pod Point has recently announced that they will be switching to a Pay as you go scheme by 2015.

    • Register for your Pod Point card

    Polar

    Charge Master Network (powered by polar energy) is one of the UK’s largest charging networks and has about 4000 charging points in the UK. There are several membership options to keep you connected. There is the option of a £20 annual subscription or simply using the pay as you go. To register for your Polar membership card: please e-mail here or telephone 01582 400 331 during office hours to obtain an application form.

    • Download the Polar charging App

    3 ways to charge

    Charging the ZOE is simple and flexible due to new chameleon charger technology. This means it can take charge from multiple types of charge points.

    The first charging method is through your home wall box, provided by British Gas and is installed before your car is delivered. This is a 7 KW charger that can fully charge the ZOE in 3-4 hours. We recommend this is where you should do the majority of your charging so that topping up the car can be effortlessly integrated into your daily routine. 

    The second way to charge is by using a public charging post, of which there are already over 3,000 nationwide . These chargers range from 3KW - 22KW and can charge your car from 8 - 1 hours respectively. This type is ideal for round trips, such as going to and from one destination. 

    The final way to charge your ZOE is through rapid charging. These appliances (43KW capacity) are placed systematically at key locations such as motorway service stations for long journeys and have the ability to charge the battery to 80% in just half an hour. With over 100 rapid chargers installed nationwide already, this growing network will allow you to put in some serious mileage with your ZOE.

    Z.E. Equipment

    Designed for everyday life.

    CHAMELEON CHARGER

    Discover the Renault-patented Chameleon charger, which is able to adapt to the various available charging power levels. This innovation allows you to choose one of three modes to charge your ZOE: Standard Charge (full charge in 6 to 9 hours), 22kW Medium Charge (80% charge in 1 hour) and 43 kW Fast Charge (80% charge in 30 minutes).

    CHARGING CABLE WITH Z.E. BAG

    The supplied charging cable allows you to carry out a standard charge at a Z.E. Ready* certified charging wall box (version 1.2 or later version and with minimum power of 3kW) or a public charge point. It also allows you to carry out a medium charge (22 kW) on a public charge point. However, you may only carry out a fast charge (43 kW) with the cable connected to public charge points.

    * Z.E. Ready is a trademark registered by Renault displayed on the wall boxes installed for the charging of electric vehicles in the Z.E. range. It contains the list of technical specifications and compliance tests to be carried out by the partner in order to be certified Z.E. Ready.

    RANGE OPTIMIZER: HEAT PUMP

    One system for two functions: to refresh the passenger compartment, the system works according to the same principle as standard modern air conditioning. To warm it up, the system works in reverse in order to capture the ambient heat in the air outside the vehicle. You thus minimise the energy drained from the battery and preserve your vehicle's driving range.

    RANGE OPTIMIZER: REGENERATIVE BRAKING

    The battery charges with every deceleration, when you lift your foot off the accelerator and when you press the brake pedal.

    RANGE OPTIMIZER: ENERGY™ E-V MICHELIN TYRES

    Enjoy ultra-low rolling resistance without modifying braking capacity or road-holding!

    PASSENGER COMPARTMENT PRE-CONDITIONING

    Programme the heating or air-conditioning whilst your ZOE is still charging. This way, the energy used for thermal comfort is not drained from the battery.

    ECO MODE

    An 'Eco' button lets you reduce the car's dynamic performance and the power of the air-conditioning or heating to extend your driving range.

    ECONOMETER

    Thanks to the econometer located on the screen behind the steering wheel, you can view your energy consumption in real-time. The screen changes colour according to your driving style and you can customise it according to 3 configurations.
