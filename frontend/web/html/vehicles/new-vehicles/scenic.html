    SCENIC LIMITED

    Limited edition

    Magnetic styling
    Comfort and safety

    Magnetic styling

    In terms of looks, Scénic Limited stands out with its 16" wheels and LED daytime running lights. The tinted windows and glossy black door mirrors enhance the colour of the car. A chrome-finish badge and 'LIMITED' doorway add an elegant finishing touch.

    Comfort and safety

    Scénic Limited is packed with useful equipment! Leather steering wheel as standard, dual-zone climate control, and rain and light sensors are there to provide the most comfortable driving experience. Scénic Limited takes your safety seriously with technology like Electronic Stability Control (ESC) and emergency brake assist.

    Power

    Designed to meet a full range of needs, Limited comes with high-performance, economical petrol and diesel engines. Choose between different power levels and a manual or automatic dual-clutch 6-speed gearbox.

    Expect More…

    With Renault Limited, enjoy a range of privileges: upgraded equipment, a distinctive exterior look, and an even more attractive price.
    Upgraded models are even more elegant with specific design features, such as alloy wheels and dark-tinted windows…

    Renault Scenic Bose®+ pack

    Bose®+ pack

    Exterior design

    Welcoming, intelligent and responsive, Renault Scénic Bose® combines sporty elegance with a unique sound experience.

    Interior design

    The large interior features Teflon® protected upholstery, a perforated leather* steering wheel and leather gear knob*, all featuring grey top-stitching.
    Other equipment such as the sliding central storage console and Renault R-Link multimedia system are also standard.

    * Distressed leather.

    THE GOLD STANDARD IN SOUND

    Renault has chosen Bose® to bring you a live music experience. With its eight high-performance speakers controlled by a digital amplifier, the sound is natural, rich and full!

    A partnership in sound, for measurable results

    Since 2008, Renault and Bose® have collaborated to bring motorists all the benefits of the two brands: innovation, pleasure and quality, shared expertise, dialogue and exchange... A rehearsal period that played a key role in efforts to bring you the very best acoustic experience!
