    PERFORMANCE

    Taking you even further

    Available with a choice of four Energy engines, Scénic XMOD is leader for driving pleasure. Featuring cutting-edge technology, the Energy engines combine driving sensations with savings and respect for the environment.

    FOR MORE INFORMATION

    • ENERGY TCe 130 with overboost
    • Energy dCi 130
    • Energy TCe 115
    • Energy dCi 130

    ENERGY TCe 130 with overboost

    The new Energy TCe 130 combines power, response and performance. It is also economical, silent and a pleasure to drive.

    This turbocharged direct-injection petrol engine is packed with technologies, delivering performance and fuel efficiency, based on the expertise and know-how of Renault's engine specialists.

    With peak power of 130hp, the Energy TCe 130 delivers a powerful and smooth response at all revs: from 1,600rpm to a maximum of over 4,500rpm.
    The 205Nm of torque (at 2,000rpm) rises to 225Nm with the overboost function, for even better performance and response. With the overboost function, the Energy TCe 130 gains increased torque (+ 20Nm) for pick-up and strong acceleration This strong, reassuring pick-up is ideal for overtaking or slipping into the traffic.

    A versatile engine, the Energy TCe 130 adapts to driving in the city (smooth, comfortable and adaptable) and on the open road (performance and pick-up).

    Energy dCi 130

    A powerful diesel engine achieving 130hp. Versatile and efficient, the Energy dCi 130 consumes just 4.4l/100km* for CO2 emissions of 114g per km*.
    With generous torque of 320Nm, available from 1,750rpm across a broad band or revs, driving is a real pleasure on board Scénic Xmod Energy dCi 130. Enjoy a relaxing drive with this quietly discreet engine and smooth Stop & Start function.

    Impressive results based on the know-how and expertise of our engine specialists.

    Energy TCe 115

    Renault's first turbocharged direct-injection petrol engine, the Energy TCe 115 consumes just 6.1l/100 km for CO2 emissions of 140g/km over a combined cycle*.
    It is a 1.2l (1,198 cc) turbocharged direct-injection unit with an aluminium block, four cylinders and 16 valves.
    It achieves 115hp for generous maximum torque (torque = pick-up and acceleration) of 190Nm, available between 2,000 and 4,000rpm - 90% from 1,500rpm. The output of 100hp per litre is exceptional for a 1.2 petrol engine.

    Energy dCi 130

    The Energy dCi 110, engine combines technology with fuel efficiency.
    The Stop & Start function saves more than one litre of fuel per 100 km* in the city.
    With this new engine, Scénic XMOD ranks among the market leaders for fuel efficiency and CO2 emissions
    - 3.5l/100 km* over a combined cycle
    - 90g of CO2/km*.

    In terms of performance, the new Energy dCi 110 engine develops 110hp at 4,000rpm, with strong torque of 260Nm available from 1,750rpm.
    This engine delivers an exciting drive with 80% of torque available from 1,500rpm for pick-up and acceleration!

    *Official Fuel consumption figures are produced for comparison purpose and are measured according to the European Directive 80/1268/EEC. These figures are specific for a vehicle type and not for a single car. Real world fuel (actual) consumption can vary as the fuel efficiency of a vehicle is influenced by driving behaviour, weather and road conditions. The use of technical equipment for example air conditioning, can also effect the fuel consumption. For further information, please visit the Vehicle Certification Agency web site www.vca.gov.uk.

    EDC automatic gearbox

    EDC (Efficient Dual Clutch) transmission is an integral part of Renault's strategy to combine comfort, driving pleasure with respect for the environment via the Renault eco² label. With the EDC 6-speed automatic dual clutch gearbox, fuel efficiency and CO2 emissions are comparable to those of a manual gearbox.

    DUAL CLUTCH TRANSMISSION

    The dual clutch on the EDC gearbox contributes to comfort.

    A BETTER RESPONSE FOR GREATER DRIVING PLEASURE

    The new EDC automatic gearbox is as responsive as a manual gearbox in all circumstances and in all modes (normal, sport or race). The driver can take over at any time for an even greater drive.

    ECONOMICAL AND ECOLOGICAL

    • A double dry clutch to reduce friction and thus improve efficiency.
    • Energy-efficient electric actuators controlled by the ECU.
    • Shift patterns optimised for fast change-up in order to control fuel consumption and CO2 emissions

    Chassis

    With identically sized front and rear tracks, Scénic XMOD possesses exceptional balance and directional stability.
