    Grip the road as never before

    Safety

    Stay on course and optimise traction in the most difficult driving conditions with the Grip Xtend system.

    Grip Xtend

    The Scénic XMOD will venture where others would fear to tread… The intelligent Grip Xtend traction control system provides maximum grip in difficult driving conditions. A thumbwheel positioned on the fixed centre console allows you to select 3 possible settings: "road", "loose ground" or "expert".

    ROAD MODE

    This is the best mode for hard, dry or wet surfaces, as well as for black ice. In Road mode, the ESC and ASR traction control function automatically manage directional stability and traction.

    LOOSE GROUND MODE

    This mode allows the front wheels to spin more freely. The system modifies engine torque and brakes automatically to allow greater wheel spin. In this way, the mud & snow tyres are able to clear mud or sand on the road and regain a better grip in order to move forwards.

    EXPERT MODE

    When Extended Grip is used in Expert mode, the driver controls the response to difficult driving conditions. They stay in full control of engine torque. In this mode, it is the driver's control of the accelerator pedal that will enable the car to move forward.

    Packed with technology

    Technology

    The best in high-tech innovation for comfort, convenience and easier everyday driving.

    Rain and light sensor

    The automatic speed-sensitive windscreen wipers start and adapt their rate to the intensity of the rain.
    A light sensor automatically switches on the headlights when night falls or when you enter a tunnel.

    Dual-zone climate control

    This function delivers personalised thermal comfort, enabling each passenger to select a temperature to suit. Auto mode ensures balanced temperature management, while Fast and Soft modes place the emphasis on temperature or acoustics.

    Lane departure warning and Visio System®

    A front camera positioned on the interior rearview mirror warns the driver if the car drifts out of its line or if the lighting mode changes.

    Hill-start assist

    Hill-start assist (standard) is activated automatically on slopes (from a gradient of 10%).

    Electric rearview mirrors

    On all versions, the rearview mirrors include power adjustments and a heating function.

    Heated front seats

    These seats are combined with the leather pack. The driver's seat features power adjustment and a position memory.

    Electrochrome interior rearview mirror

    This particularly ingenious mirror gradually dims as light levels increase in order to avoid dazzle.

    R-Link multimedia system

    Technology

    Renault R-Link transfers the world of smartphones to cars with its large touchscreen, modern interfaces, and the attractive graphics used for all content. Voice recognition lets you enter a destination or call any contact in your address book.

    TELEPHONE

    Find all your contacts automatically, simply use the sound of your voice to call them.

     

    NAVIGATION

    Take advantage of the best real-time traffic information system to calculate your journey time and make travelling easier with TomTom and its LIVE services: HD Traffic®

    SERVICES & VEHICLE

    Access the complete catalogue of Renault R-Link apps in your Scénic XMOD.

     

    A generous interior

    Stowage

    Find a place for all your belongings in the many ingenious storage compartments at the front and rear. The modular interior layout lets you fold or remove the three rear seats as required.
