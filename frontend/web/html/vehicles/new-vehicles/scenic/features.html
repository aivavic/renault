    RELAXED DRIVING

    Safety

    Bi-xenon directional headlights

    Optimise control and anticipation onboard your Scénic with a light that is similar to daylight. The beam follows the bends in the road for greater precision and control.

    Anti-lock Braking System (ABS) and Electronic Stability Control (ESC)

    To ensure your safety, Scénic features ABS and ESC. These features are combined with the ASR anti-skid system and understeer control function, to ensure your safety and that of your passengers in even the toughest conditions.

    Protecting vehicle occupants

    Scénic comes with a full set of airbags and side impact sensors. The Fix4sure system keeps you safe by preventing submarining. It also ensures your comfort with a special hump in the cushion of each seat. Drive safely with Renault!

    Visio-System® by Renault

    This front camera provides two driving assistance functions:

    Automatic main beam headlights: adjust between main and dipped beam, depending on conditions.

    Lane departure warning: warns the driver when the vehicle crosses a broken or unbroken lane marking, without indicating.

    Large comfortable headrests

    The height and angle can be adjusted to prevent the risk of whiplash.

    Additional fog lamps

    Improve your visibility in foggy or rainy conditions.

    Hill-start assist

    Hill-start assist makes for an easier drive on steep slopes, whether uphill or downhill. It is standard on all trim levels and for all engines.

    Programmed restraint system

    Enjoy a relaxed drive with a full set of airbags, side impact sensors and an anti-submarining function.

    COMFORT & CONVENIENCE

    Comfort

    A practical mindset

    All passengers have handy and accessible storage compartments within easy reach (up to 86 litres): drawers* under seats and flaps beneath the floor, an 11 litre cooled glove compartment, a sliding central storage compartment**, a cup holder, door compartments, map pockets*, and more. Plenty of space to store all your belongings.

    * Depending on version.
    ** Optional.

    A true living area

    With Scénic, ergonomic design takes on a new meaning. Discover the grand comfort headrests*, exclusive to Scénic. Lateral and height adjustment make for a level of comfort worthy of first class travel. The heated power seats** memorise up to three settings to quickly adjust to each driver.

    * Optional.
    ** Depending on version.

    Your passengers can relax

    Scénic takes care of its passengers: generous headroom and legroom mean that even tall adults can travel in comfort.

    Modular layout

    Fold and unfold the seats as required. It's so simple! With loading capacity of 555dm3* (1,870dm3* with the seats removed), a spur-of-the-moment weekend trip or a small move are no longer a problem!

    Dual-zone climate control

    Enjoy personalised thermal comfort with fully automatic dual-zone climate control. Scénic lets you select the ideal temperature from three modes. Auto mode ensures balanced temperature management, while Fast or Soft modes place the emphasis on temperature or acoustics.

    Rain and light sensor

    Are you absent-minded? Renault's engineers have just what you need, since Scénic comes with a rain and light sensor. The automatic speed control windscreen wipers start and adapt their sweep rate to the intensity of the rain. A light sensor automatically switches on the headlamps when night falls or when you enter a tunnel.

    Sunroof

    Enjoy a panoramic view and a bright, airy passenger compartment with the electric roof!

    Hands-free card

    Remote access, start-up and locking. Keyless.

    Front seat

    For greater comfort, we have equipped Scénic with heated seats (power on driver’s side) with the Leather Pack. Seats are height-adjustable with lumbar control (on driver’s side).

    Teflon treatment

    Renault is the first car maker to market a Teflon® treated textile carrying the 'Automotive High Protection' label. This stain-repellent coating on the upholstery repels water and oil-based liquids for easier cleaning.

    Teflon® is a trademark of E.I. DuPont de Nemours and Company.

    Large comfortable headrests

    Treat yourself to first-class comfort! Headrests feature lateral, height and tilt adjustment for optimum comfort.

    Electronic parking brake

    The parking brake is deactivated automatically when the engine is running, a gear is engaged or when the clutch is released. It is activated when the engine is turned off and it can be manually controlled with a simple touch.

    Fuel flap with integrated cap

    No more getting your hands dirty! The fuel flap has an integrated opening, so there is no cap to unscrew and replace on the Renault Scénic.

    Multimedia for all

    Technology

    CRUISE CONTROL - SPEED LIMITER

    The cruise control-speed limiter function lets you set a cruising speed or maximum speed.
    A control function on the steering column lets you adjust the speed selected.

    FRONT AND REAR PARKING DISTANCE CONTROL / REVERSING CAMERA

    Make life easier! Parking distance controls warn you of any obstacles by emitting a visual and sound signal.

    One-touch indicators

    When changing lanes, flicking the indicator switch activates three consecutive flash signals.

    BOSE®+ pack equipment level

    The Bose® Energy Efficient Series sound system was designed and developed by Bose® especially for Scénic and Grand Scénic. Adapted especially for vehicle interior acoustics, the sound is custom-made, from the front seats to the rear seats.

    R-LINK

    Technology

    R-LINK

    Renault R-Link brings you:

    • A large 18cm (7") touchscreen,
    • TomTom® navigation,
    • Innovative voice control system allowing you to call a contact
    • Reversing camera (optional),
    • Internet connection offering a wide choice of applications for download, such as Coyote® and TomTom® LIVE.

    Simple features

    With Renault R-Link, enjoy simple functions suited to the vehicle. With Bluetooth® and the USB ports and jack, it is easy to listen to your playlists or to view your photos and videos when stationary. From the car or from your computer, you can download a wide range of applications.

    Navigation

    Discover TomTom® connected navigation and the fantastic services it has to offer including LIVE:HD Traffic®, which collects the best traffic information in real time and uses it to calculate your estimated driving time and improve your route. LIVE takes into account all the latest traffic bulletins, any road safety warnings, local-area searches and weather forecasts.

    R-link - Services & vehicles

    Use the pre-installed apps (email, Renault R-Link, Tweet, weather, Renault Assistance) and also access the complete catalogue of Renault R-Link apps from your Scénic.

Providing all the functions you would expect from an excellent GPS system. Alongside the IQ Routes function, enjoy LIVE Services, a technology exclusive to TomTom.

    Map pockets and aircraft-type trays

    Enjoy plenty of storage space with Renault Scénic!
    With two small pockets for crayons or pencils and a large pocket for books and games. Your children will love it!

    Scenic boot

    Capacity is between 470 and 1870dm3 (ISO 3832 standards and with repair kit).

    Storage in the central console

    Clever and practical, Scénic comes with a variety of storage areas (up to 86l!), including a 8l sliding console. The two compartments let you store all your belongings, from the largest item to the smallest. Perfect for keeping the essentials within easy reach.

    Advanced comfort driving position

    Enjoy customizable comfort on board the Scénic. The driving position adapts to your body shape with a seat adjustment range of up to 7cm in height and the steering wheel adjustable for height and reach.

    Drawers under seat

    Conceal your belongings from prying eyes using the drawers (4.5l each) located under the two front seats and two side seats.

    Flaps under the floor

    Flaps under the floor to store your belongings! The two front seats and two side seats provide capacity of up to 14l to store bulkier items.
