    New Energy Tce 130

    Energy Tce130

    Based on the experience and know-how of Renault engineers, the new Energy TCe 130 is a turbocharged direct-injection petrol engine! With output of 130hp, it delivers a dynamic responsive ride at all speeds. The 205Nm of torque (at 2,000rpm), increasing to 225Nm with the overboost function, gives it boundless energy!

    Energy TCe 115

    Energy TCe115

    Renault's first turbocharged direct-injection petrol engine!
    It combines output of 115hp with excellent peak torque (torque = pick-up and acceleration) of 190Nm, available between 2,000 and 4,000rpm.
    With its environmentally-friendly design, this engine delivers record fuel efficiency and CO2 emissions.

    Energy dCi 110

    Energy dCi 110

    An efficient engine, economical to buy and to use, the Energy dCi 110 is packed with technological innovation. The ongoing aim of Renault engineers is to improve the engine output while reducing fuel consumption (in litres of fuel) and CO2 emissions, with no change in horsepower. With this engine, Scénic achieves around 57mpg* (over a combined cycle) for just 105g of CO2/km*!

    Energy dCi 130

    Energy dCi 130

    With the Energy dCi 130 engine, Scénic gains a powerful 130hp diesel engine that is both versatile and ultra-efficient. It achieves around 53mpg* and emits just 114g CO2/km*.

    *Official Fuel consumption figures are produced for comparison purpose and are measured according to the European Directive 80/1268/EEC. These figures are specific for a vehicle type and not for a single car. Real world fuel (actual) consumption can vary as the fuel efficiency of a vehicle is influenced by driving behaviour, weather and road conditions. The use of technical equipment for example air conditioning, can also effect the fuel consumption. For further information, please visit the Vehicle Certification Agency web site www.vca.gov.uk.

    Comfort, fuel efficiency and responsive performance

    The comfort of an automatic gearbox, with the economy and reactivity of a manual gearbox. EDC (Efficient Dual Clutch) transmission is an integral part of Renault's strategy to combine comfort, pleasurable driving with respect for the environment via the Renault eco² label. With the EDC 6-speed automatic dual-clutch gearbox, fuel efficiency and CO2 emissions are comparable with those of a manual gearbox.

    comfort

    The comfort of an automatic gearbox!

    The EDC 6-speed automatic dual-clutch gearbox comes with exceptional features:

    • A jolt-free torque change. With the EDC, changing up is smooth and easy, with no loss of acceleration
    • Optimised 'creep' for a smoother drive, useful in traffic jams and tricky manoeuvres.
    • Hill-start assist, a reassuring function to hold the vehicle steady for a few seconds before acceleration.

    fuel-efficiency

    Technological decisions were guided by a quest for fuel-efficiency in a manual gearbox:

    • A double dry clutch to reduce friction and thus improve efficiency.
    • Energy-efficient electric actuators controlled by the ECU.
    • Shift patterns optimised for fast change-up in order to control fuel consumption and CO2 emissions.

    responsiveness

    The new EDC automatic gearbox is as responsive as a manual gearbox in all circumstances:

    • Instant response to driver input, in both automatic and flick-shift mode.
    • Flick-shift mode lets the driver override the automatic system. The driver is in control of upward or downward gear changes at all times for the best drive.
