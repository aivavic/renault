    Features

    Characteristics

    In terms of equipment, the Mégane Sport Tourer leads the way! From the Renault R-Link multimedia system to the the hands-free card, stay in control and make the most of your journey!

    • Comfort and well-being
    • Storage and modular layout
    • Technology
    • Safety

    PERSONALISED COMFORT

    Comfort and well-being

    Dual-zone climate control

    Enjoy personalised thermal comfort with fully automatic dual-zone air conditioning. The Mégane Sport Tourer lets you select your preferred temperature with three modes.

    • Auto mode ensures balanced temperature control.
    • The Fast and Soft modes favour either temperature or acoustics.

    Rain and light sensor

    We wanted to simplify your driving experience with a rain and light sensor. The wipers start and adapt their rate according to the intensity of the rain. Our engineers have also installed a light sensor that automatically switches on the lights when night falls or when you enter a tunnel.

    Heated front seats

    For greater comfort, we have equipped the Mégane Sport Tourer with heated seats (power on driver's side) with the Leather Pack. The seats are height-adjustable with lumbar control (on driver's side).

    Set-up menu

    Using the customisation menu, adjust your preferences to control the display and settings of various vehicle functions (navigation, radio, etc.).

    Wing mirror

    Electric, demisting and electric folding.

    PRACTICALITIES

    Storage and modular layout

    Boot

    With its multiple ingenious and practical storage areas, its useful technology and its 1/3-2/3 folding bench seat, the New Mégane Sport Tourer will surpass all of your expectations. If you have children to take with you, bags to transport or items to be moved, the luggage compartment volume transforms instantly from 524 dm3, to 1,600 dm3. Much more than a simple estate car. Opt for a car that's both practical and pleasing with this true partner for everyday life.

    Central console storage

    An easy-access storage area for you and your passenger.

    Front door compartments

    Practical storage compartments! Use them to store CDs, your diary, etc.

    Glove compartment

    Offering generous volume, the illuminated glovebox locks and unlocks at the same time as the vehicle doors.

    PLEASURE AT YOUR FINGERTIPS

    Technology

    Leading the way in making your journey simpler.

    Multimedia

    A true onboard computer, R-Link efficiently manages all of your vehicle's multimedia functions and your phone calls.

    R-Link has every essential type of connectivity so you can access all of your content at any time (Bluetooth, USB, SD or Jack).

    R-Link Store

    Weather, Email, Renault Assistance - a selection of apps is available by default in Renault R-Link.

    Want more? Nothing could be simpler: after registering on My Renault, you can search the entire R-Link Store catalogue for the app you need.

    Cruise control - speed limiter

    The cruise control-speed limiter function lets you set a cruising speed or maximum speed.
    A control function on the steering column lets you adjust the speed selected.

    Reversing camera

    The reversing camera makes manoeuvring easier. The image is shown on the navigation system screen and displays guiding lines. It supplements the audible and visual assistance provided by the front and rear sensors.

    Digital instrument cluster

    Keep control of your journey thanks to the three reading levels on the digital instrument cluster.

    RADIOSAT 3D SOUND BY ARKAMYS BLUETOOTH®

    The digital sound processing, made by Arkamys for the Mégane, allows the eight speakers to play high-quality 3D sound. This gives you acoustic comfort and optimised, balanced, surround sound.

    Voice recognition

    Make calls, find a route or even open an app easily and safely using your voice.

    The Text to speech function* completes the picture by automatically reading out your text messages, emails and RSS feeds whilst you are driving.

    STATE OF THE ART SAFETY

    Safety

    Visio System®

    This system is equipped with a camera that scans the road in real-time. It detects road markings, alerts you when you cross a continuous line and automatically adjusts the car's lights. An invaluable tool!

    Seat belt

    To reduce force in the event of a collision and keep the driver and passengers in their seats, the seat belts are fitted with dual pretensioners in the front seats and pretensioners with force limiters in the rear side seats.

    ABS

    Keep control of the trajectory when braking firmly thanks to the ABS anti-lock braking system.

    ANTI-WHIPLASH FRONT HEADRESTS

    Adjust the height and inclination of your headrests to prevent whiplash.

    Front and rear parking distance control

    This system facilitates manoeuvres and allows you to park more quickly and easily.

    Tyre pressure monitoring system

    This system warns you immediately if you have a puncture or a pressure fault to prevent any risk of you losing control of the car.

    Electronic Stability Control (ESC)

    The new-generation ESC (Electronic Stability Control) and anti-skid system (ASR) keep the vehicle stable, including in difficult road conditions.

    Protecting vehicle occupants

    Reinforced structure:

    • High-efficiency front airbags
    • Head/chest side airbags
    • Pressure sensor to detect impact
    • Force limiter function (rear seats)
    • Luggage restraint crossmember
    • Anti-submarining front and rear seats (Fix4sure)
    • 3 Isofix seat attachments.

    Hill-start assist

    Take your time when starting! When starting off on a hill, the brake pressure is automatically maintained for two seconds to allow you the time to move off calmly.
