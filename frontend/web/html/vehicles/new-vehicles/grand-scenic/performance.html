    PERFORMANCE

    Dynamic, economical and fun

    The new range of Energy engines achieve a perfect blend of ecology and economy whilst still meeting your performance needs. As for road-holding, Grand Scénic maintains its legendary stability, ensuring full driving pleasure.

    ENERGY TCe 130 with overboost

    Petrol Engine

    Discover the TCe 130, an exciting turbocharged direct-injection petrol engine:

    - Its 130hp deliver a powerful and smooth response at low revs from 1,600 rpm just as at full speed above 4,500 rpm.
    - The 205Nm of torque (at 2,000 rpm), which rises to 225Nm with the Overboost function, packs a real punch. This new engine tuning makes the turbocharger kick in faster and more frequently. Your engine thus offers more torque (+20Nm) during restarts and firm accelerations. 

    The TCe 130 with Overboost is therefore perfect in any situation where you need real power, from towing a trailer to scaling mountain roads.

    Energy dCi 130

    Diesel engine

    Versatile, powerful and super-efficient, this diesel engine consumes just 4.4L/100km* and emits only 114g of CO2/km*. To achieve these impressive results, our engine specialists have combined an array of technologies:

    - Low-pressure EGR (Exhaust Gas Recirculation), which circulates exhaust fumes in the turbo for better engine performance.

    - Thermomanagement, which kicks in during cold starts to warm up the engine more quickly.

    - Variable Swirl, which adjusts the circulation of air entering the engine for optimum combustion in all conditions.

    Energy TCe 115

    Petrol engines

    The Energy TCe 115 is 1.2L (1,198 cc) direct-injection unit with an aluminium block, four cylinders and 16 valves. It achieves excellent maximum torque of 190Nm, available between 2,000 and 4,000 rpm, 90% of which is delivered at 1,500 rpm. Its 100hp per litre is exceptional for a 1.2L petrol engine. In addition to performance, this means Grand Scénic Energy TCe 115 consumes just 5.9L/100km* with emissions of 135g of CO2/km* (combined cycle).

    This combination of power and low fuel consumption is made possible by the introduction of new technologies. The torque available at low speeds is ensured by the alliance of direct injection, dual camshaft variable valve timing and supercharging delivered by an integrated turbo manifold. Optimised fuel consumption, meanwhile, is achieved by reduced friction and a variable-displacement oil pump.

    Energy dCi 110

    Diesel engine

    Powerful and economical both to buy and use, Grand Scénic's Energy dCi 110 engine boasts fuel consumption of 4.1L/100km* (combined cycle) and emissions of just 105g of CO2/km*! With 110hp (= 81kW) at 4,000 rpm, it benefits from improved torque of 260Nm available from 1,750 rpm, 80% of which is available at 1,500 rpm.

    The engine offers reduced load losses thanks to an innovative turbo architecture, ensuring power and fuel economy. The path taken by intake air has been simplified to achieve more efficient turbo performance without increasing fuel consumption. Boasting very low inertia, this turbo has enhanced response time at low speeds thanks to improved turbine sizing.

    With the new Energy dCi 110 engine and Stop & Start technology, Grand Scénic guarantees market-leading performance in fuel consumption and emissions.

    *Official Fuel consumption figures are produced for comparison purpose and are measured according to the European Directive 80/1268/EEC. These figures are specific for a vehicle type and not for a single car. Real world fuel (actual) consumption can vary as the fuel efficiency of a vehicle is influenced by driving behaviour, weather and road conditions. The use of technical equipment for example air conditioning, can also effect the fuel consumption. For further information, please visit the Vehicle Certification Agency web site www.vca.gov.uk.

    EDC automatic gearbox

    EDC (Efficient Dual Clutch) transmission is an integral part of our strategy to combine comfort, driving pleasure and respect for the environment via the Renault Eco² label.

    DUAL-CLUTCH TRANSMISSION

    The dry dual–clutch limits friction and enhances the efficiency of this automatic gearbox, providing you with reduced fuel consumption and CO2 emissions.

    ECONOMICAL AND ECOLOGICAL

    The EDC gearbox has the fuel economy of a manual gearbox. Its shift patterns have been optimised for fast gear changes and controlled fuel consumption and CO2 emissions.

    A BETTER RESPONSE FOR GREATER DRIVING PLEASURE

    In all circumstances, the new EDC gearbox instantly responds to driver input in both automatic and flick-shift mode. Flick-shift allows you to override the automatic system. You are in control of upward or downward gear changes at all times for maximum driving pleasure !
