    CHARACTERISTICS

    Grand Scenic

    With Grand Scénic, you can drive the whole family – and everything they need – in complete safety.

    • Safety
    • Multimedia
    • Comfort
    • On-board experience

    Take to the road with complete peace of mind

    Safety

    Bi-Xenon, ESC, ABS, Fix4Sure: safety has a language all of its own, born from our expertise combined with technological breakthroughs.

    Bi-xenon directional headlights

    The light emitted is similar to daylight, illuminating bends in the road by following the movement of the steering wheel over more than 20 degrees.

    Additional fog lights

    Improve visibility in foggy or rainy conditions.

    Anti-lock braking and electronic stability program

    The anti-lock braking (ABS), electronic stability program (ESP) and CSV understeer control systems keep your vehicle stable in difficult driving conditions.

    Protecting vehicle occupants

    For your safety, Grand Scénic has a full set of airbags and side impact sensors. In the event of a collision, our Fix4Sure system stops you sliding underneath the seat belt, reducing the risk of serious injury.

    Anti-whiplash head restraints

    Adjust the height and angle to suit you and prevent the risk of whiplash.

    Visio-System® by Renault

    At speeds exceeding 45kph, the camera detects the lights of other vehicles and any illuminated areas. It automatically switches between dipped and main beam headlights. The lane departure warning system detects when you cross a broken or unbroken line (above 70kph). You are alerted by light and sound signals. 

    Hill-start assist

    Thanks to this function, there is no longer any need to worry about starting on steep slopes.

    Programmed restraint system

    For your safety, Renault has equipped Grand Scénic with a full set of airbags, side impact sensors and an anti-submarining system.

    One-touch indicators

    Change lanes safely: flicking the indicator switch activates three consecutive flash signals.

    Cruise control - speed limiter

    Program your cruising or maximum speed for more pleasant, less tiring journeys.

    PLUNGE INTO THE R-LINK UNIVERSE

    Multimedia

    Renault R-Link: where the worlds of smartphones and cars combine. With its large touchscreen, modern interface, attractive graphics for all content and an extensive catalogue of apps, R-Link will soon become your faithful driving companion.

    TELEPHONE

    With R-Link, making phone calls is as easy as 1, 2, 3. Once your contacts have been synchronised, you can make calls simply by using voice commands!

    NAVIGATION

    TomTom and HD Traffic® are the ultimate combination, providing real-time traffic and weather information to make travelling easier.

    Services & Vehicles

    Browse the Renault R-Link catalogue to find the application you need and benefit from a bespoke service: email, weather, R-Link Tweet, and more.

    BOSE®: The gold standard in sound

    We know you like Bose® audio technology, which is why Renault has equipped Grand Scénic with a system of eight high-performance speakers optimised for in-car sound. The rich, natural, full sound will be music to your ears.

    Carminat TomTom®

    Multimedia

    The first integrated onboard navigation system connected in real time: Carminat TomTom® provides all the functions you would expect from an excellent GPS system! New ergonomic design makes navigation even more enjoyable.

    Create the Carminat TOMTOM® you need

    TomTom Home provides a wide range of services so you can customise your Carminat TomTom®. Whether it's new maps, a speed camera subscription or funny voices you're looking for, you will find everything you need at attractive prices.

    Make navigation even more enjoyable

    Discover a smoother, more attractive, more legible screen with easier input. Make life easier with Carminat TomTom®!

    Enjoy travelling

    Comfort

    Discover our range of accessories and features that come as standard, making every journey as comfortable as can be.

    Rain and light sensor

    You will never have to worry about your windscreen wipers again, or risk leaving your lights off with this sensor!

    Dual-zone climate control

    Automatic dual-zone climate control allows each front seat passenger to select their desired temperature.

    Electrochrome interior rearview mirror

    The mirror gradually dims as light levels increase to avoid dazzle, so that you can stay focused on the road.

    Side air vents

    Rear passengers can electronically adjust their ventilation for maximum comfort.

    FRONT SEAT

    Heated with the Leather Pack, and height and lumbar-adjustable for maximum comfort.

    Large comfortable headrests

    Enjoy first-class comfort with these fully adjustable headrests.

    Discover Teflon treating

    Grand Scénic will stay as clean as on the day you bought it with the Teflon® Automotive High Protection treatment. It repels water and oil-based liquids for easier cleaning.

    Technology at your service

    Comfort

    Travel in supreme comfort thanks to equipment that is designed to make your life easier.

    CLEAR VIEW FUNCTION

    Demist or de-ice your windows in a flash.

    Hands-free card

    Remote access, start-up and locking.

    Fuel flap with integrated cap

    The petrol cap is already integrated into the flap, allowing you to fill up without getting dirty or losing sight of it. Smart, isn't it?

    Electronic parking brake

    Turn off the engine and the electronic parking brake is applied. Start the engine or engage a gear and it automatically deactivates. Of course, you can also control it manually using a button located near the gear stick.

    REAR PARKING DISTANCE CONTROL

    Let the system guide you: a sound signal warns you of obstacles behind the car, and the reversing camera shows the available space on the R-Link screen. The trickiest parallel parking will be a breeze!

    Spacious and practical

    Onboard experience

    Modular and practical, Grand Scénic offers an impressive loading capacity of 564dm3. Need more space? Remove the seats and increase the volume to 2,063dm3. Whether you're taking the family on holiday or moving house, space will never again be an issue!

    Your passengers can relax

    Comfortable, spacious and modular, Grand Scénic caters to everyone. Passengers large and small can all travel in comfort!

    Sunroof

    The sunroof offers a panoramic view and lets light flood in for all passengers.

    A practical mindset

    Grand Scénic has a vast array of practical storage compartments. There is space for all of your belongings, whether under a flap, in a drawer or door compartment, or inside the 11L cooled glove compartment.

    A true living area

    With Grand Scénic, ergonomic design takes on a whole new meaning. Relax and enjoy first-class comfort!

    Storage and modular layout

    Onboard experience

    Take all your belongings on board – Grand Scénic is packed with clever, accessible storage areas.

    Map pockets and aircraft-type trays

    Your children will love the seatback trays. We bet they'll quickly turn them into their own play area!

    SPACIOUS BOOT

    Grand Scénic's boot offers an impressive capacity of 470 to 1,870 dm3!

    Storage in the central console

    Bottles, sandwiches, smartphones, etc. Keep all your essentials within reach with this sliding console with 8L capacity.

    Advanced comfort driving position

    Whether you have a small or large frame, the driving position is designed to adapt to all body types.

    Under-seat storage

    These 4.5L drawers allow your passengers to travel with all of their day-to-day items (books, pens, snacks and other essentials).

    Flaps under the floor

    Use the 14 litres of space provided by these flaps located in front of the two front and rear side seats to tidy away larger items such as portable games consoles, glasses cases, etc.
