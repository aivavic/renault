    Adventurer Pack

    Get ready to look good in the great outdoors

    1. Side Embellishers: Enhance the design of your new car with the chrome side embellishers.

    2. Transversal Roof bars: Tested to the highest standards, our roof bars are guaranteed to fit your car perfectly.  Quick and easy fitting, allows you to carry your roof box, cycle carriers, etc. (please note, roof box sold separately)

    3. Boot protector: protects the loading area from dirt, scrapes and scuffs.

    Touring Pack

    Everything you need to hit the open road with your caravan or trailer.

    1. Transversal roof bars: Tested to the highest standards, our roof bars are guaranteed to fit your car perfectly.  Quick and easy fitting, allows you to carry your roof box, cycle carriers, etc. (please note, roof box sold separately)

    2. Swan neck tow bar with 7-pin electric kit: This tow bar, designed, developed and tested by Renault, offers reliability, high strength and durability.  It therefore ensures total compatibility with the vehicle, avoiding any risk of damaging the vehicle body.

    3. Luggage boot net: This easy-to-install boot net will stop your possessions rolling around inside your boot (not shown).
