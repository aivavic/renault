    THE BEST IN INNOVATION

    TECHNOLOGY

    LET YOUR SMARTPHONE TAKE CONTROL

    The R&Go® Smartphone application turns your phone into a touchscreen dashboard and adds navigation to your Twingo.  When connected to the vehicle via Bluetooth®, your phone's screen provides ergonomic and intuitive access to the features of your car's stereo, navigation through the application and your phone.  R&Go® is available on Apple iOS and Android


    R-LINK EVOLUTION MAKES LIFE EASIER

    Looking for easy solutions? R-LINK combines modern interfaces with attractive graphics. With its touch screen, steering-wheel controls and voice command function, R-Link is ideally suited to cars. Use TomTom® navigation to find your way, make hands-free calls using the Bluetooth® function and customize your Renault R-Link Evolution with the app catalogue on R-Link Store.

    STOP & START TECHNOLOGY

    Stop & Start cuts the engine when you come to a standstill and starts it again automatically. An ideal solution to consume less fuel, emit fewer pollutants and make less noise. The engine stops when you put the car in neutral and starts again when you let out the clutch.

    ENERGY SMART MANAGEMENT (ESM)

    Fewer emissions and better fuel consumption? ESM technology makes this possible with the Energy TCe 90 engine. ESM recovers kinetic energy and uses it to recharge the battery and optimise consumption of electrical energy.

    COMFORT AND CONVENIENCE

    COMFORT

    THE CITY IS ITS PLAYGROUND

    All-New Twingo is ideally suited to urban driving: compact and agile, it can go anywhere. Parking manoeuvres are easy, since All-New Twingo is just 3.59m long, with a record turning circle of 8.59m, rear parking sensors and reversing camera (optional)! The range of petrol engines lets you adapt your driving to any type of road.

    A SPACIOUS INTERIOR

    Although 10cm shorter than the previous Twingo, All-New Twingo is more spacious inside with an extra 22cm in the cabin! The cabin is designed to provide as much space as possible. You can carry loads of up to 2.30m, a first for a compact city car! The flat boot floor lets you make full use of capacity. Overall, All-New Twingo provides an extra 52 litres of interior storage space.

    A FUNCTIONAL INTERIOR

    A rear-mounted engine? A surprising choice for All-New Twingo, but one that maximizes onboard space. All-New Twingo features a functional, easy-access storage compartment for front passengers. Discover the generously sized illuminated glovebox that locks and unlocks at the same time as the vehicle.

    CLIMATE CONTROL

    With a cabin filter. Optional depending on versions.

    SOFT-TOP ROOF

    Open the soft-top roof using the power controls.

    automatic headlights

    The rain and light sensor turns on the wipers and dipped-beam lights automatically.

    REAR PARKING SENSORS

    Parking is easy! The function warns you of any obstacles with a series of beeps that become more frequent and then turn into a continuous tone as you move closer. The reversing camera shows you the space available for parking on your Renault R-Link screen.

    VARIABLE-ASSIST POWER STEERING

    Alongside its conventional power steering, All-New Twingo is available with variable-assist power steering with the Energy TCe 90 engine. This function turns the wheels more quickly to their maximum angle.

    HILL-START ASSIST

    Not of fan of hill starts? No more worries about slipping backwards! On a slope, All-New Twingo remains still for two seconds when you take your foot off the brake. Leaving you time to calmly move off!

    DRIVE WITH COMPLETE PEACE OF MIND

    SAFETY

    ABS ANTI-WHEEL LOCKING

    Drive safely with ABS braking assistance. If you brake suddenly, it will prevent the wheels from locking and help you keep control of steering. At the same time, emergency brake assist (EBA) will help you stay on course no matter how hard you brake.

    ESC ELECTRONIC STABILITY CONTROL

    If the car loses its grip, the ESC function helps you keep the car under control without swerving off course.

    A CRUISE CONTROL/SPEED LIMITER FOR A MORE RELAXED DRIVE

    Enjoy a more relaxed drive with the cruise control/speed limiter function. Steering-wheel controls let you set a steady driving speed (cruise control) or a maximum speed that you do not wish to exceed (speed limiter function).

    ISOFIX, SECURELY ATTACHED CHILD SEATS

    ISOFIX lets you install child seats on the two rear seats. Carry your children safely!

    STAY ON COURSE WITH THE LANE DEPARTURE WARNING FUNCTION

    Using a camera on the central rearview mirror, this function activates a light and sound alert if you drift out of your lane.

    AIRBAGS FOR ADVANCED DRIVING PROTECTION

    All-New Twingo is equipped with front and side airbags for you and your passenger. If you want to install a child seat at the front, you can easily disconnect the passenger airbag.

    TYRES UNDER CONTROL

    To prevent loss of vehicle control, a function warns you if tyre pressure is insufficient or in the event of a puncture.

    VISIBLE BY DAY AND NIGHT

    All-New Twingo is equipped with LED lights to make sure that you can be seen easily from a distance or in sunlight. In the event of poor weather, the fog lights improve visibility. Standard on some versions.
