    PERFORMANCE

    All-New Twingo adapts to your needs to deliver real driving pleasure. A city slicker, a savvy saver or an adventurer? Choose the engine that's right for you!

    FOR MORE INFORMATION ON

    • Sce70 engine

    Sce70 engine

    IDEAL FOR THE CITY

    City slicker? With this 70 hp engine, your All-New Twingo is built for the city. It delivers a lively, smooth and comfortable drive.

    WITH STOP & START

    Savvy saver? With consumption of 67.2mpg (over a combined cycle)* and 95g of CO2/km, the Sce70 Stop & Start petrol engine cuts fuel consumption while respecting the environment. The 5-speed manual gearbox is designed for smooth gear-changing.

    *Official Fuel consumption figures are produced for comparison purpose and are measured according to the European Directive 80/1268/EEC. These figures are specific for a vehicle type and not for a single car. Real world fuel (actual) consumption can vary as the fuel efficiency of a vehicle is influenced by driving behaviour, weather and road conditions. The use of technical equipment for example air conditioning, can also effect the fuel consumption. For further information, please visit the Vehicle Certification Agency web site www.vca.gov.uk.

    ENERGY TCE90 ENGINE

    Adventurer? The TCe90 petrol engine is built for you. The turbocharger and 5-speed manual gearbox deliver crisp pick-up and acceleration. Variable-assistance power steering makes your All-New Twingo even more agile on the road.

    For those out of town, the ENERGY TCe 90 turbo petrol engine is most at home.

    With more power (90 hp) and torque (135 Nm) it is very comfortable with brisk starts and nippy acceleration. With standard Stop & Start, your driving is fun and economical.
