    Features

    CHARACTERISTICS

    All-New Renault Trafic and you

    All-New Trafic has even more surprises in store.

    • Safety
    • Comfort
    • Business features

    Improved safety

    Safety

    New-generation ESC ensures the stability of the vehicle and helps to set it back on course. It is enhanced by three key functions for your daily use:

    • GripXtend allows the vehicle to regain traction on loose ground such as sand, mud or snow.
    • Hill-start assist.
    • the Trailer Swing Assist function detects and corrects trailer sway.

    REVERSING CAMERA - SENSOR

    Manoeuvring is no longer an issue, thanks to the reversing camera combined systematically with the rear parking sensor.

    The image is relayed to the interior rearview mirror or the navigation screen for versions equipped with R-Link.

    PASSIVE SAFETY

    • Reinforced structure
    • Front programmed-restraint airbags for driver and passenger
    • Seat belts with pretensioners and load limiters
    • Side, curtain and thorax airbags (depending on the version).

    CRUISE CONTROL

    This function lets you set or adjust a maximum speed using the controls on the steering column.

    HILL-START ASSIST

    On slopes of more than 10%, the system automatically holds the car steady for 2 seconds to allow you to move off smoothly.

    FRONT FOG LIGHTS

    Improve your visibility in foggy or rainy conditions.

    TYRE PRESSURE MONITORING SYSTEM

    If pressure is lost, this system will inform you via the sensor located in the inflation valve of each wheel.

    PASSENGER AIRBAG DISCONNECTION

    You can install a child seat in the front if the airbag is deactivated.

    DOUBLE ASPHERIC DOOR MIRROR

    This improves visibility for easier manoeuvring.

    Comfort in the cab

    Comfort

    Seats built for comfort with specific design. The adjustable seats and steering wheel let everybody find the right driving position.

    90L OF STORAGE IN THE CAB

    Need storage space? All-New Renault Trafic comes with even more useful solutions, including 14 storage compartments of which one 54l unit is ideal for bulky items.

    R&GO RADIO CONNECT

    This radio allows use of the Bluetooth® hands-free telephone feature. It includes a USB port and a jack on the front panel, and a second USB port located close to the smartphone holder.

    All of these connections can stream music from mobile multimedia devices.

    Compatible with the smartphone holder (optional).

    MEDIANAV: 7" NAVIGATION TOUCHSCREEN

    Complete multimedia system with MEDIANAV, mixing business with pleasure:

    • 7" touchscreen
    • Radio with Bluetooth® technology for hands-free calls
    • USB ports and jack
    • Nav&GO 2D and 2.5D (Birdview) navigation
    • NAVTEQ mapping data update from a computer

    A DOCK FOR YOUR LAPTOP

    If needed, a storage space in the central seat back can be used to store your laptop (up to 17").
    A removable clipboard to hold A4-format documents.

    R&GO APPLICATION WITH COPILOT® PREMIUM

    Looking for music, contact management, navigation, information on fuel consumption and the Driving eco2 functions?
    Nothing could be easier with the Renault R&GO application.

     

    Available on Apple App Store (iOS) and Google Play (Android).

    AIR CONDITIONING

    Manual or automatic climate control for high standards of comfort inside the cab (standard or optional depending on the version).

    TABLET DOCK

    Access your tablet easily with this dock for tablets measuring between 4.8 and 10.6 inches.

    SMARTPHONE DOCK

    Your mobile phone will always be within easy reach thanks to the tilting holder and nearby connection/charging point.

    A MADE-TO-MEASURE RANGE

    Business features

    All-New Renault Trafic comes in up to 270 versions to address any business challenge.

    HEAVY DUTY PLY LINING

    Protect the interior of your All-New Trafic with our hardwearing, bespoke ply lining. The price includes fitting by Renault. Our lining kits are fitted without any alteration/drilling to the vehicle structure, ensuring that the paint and anti-corrosion warranties remain intact.

    Anchor RINGS

    To secure the cargo area, All-New Trafic is available with up to 18 anchorage rings, depending on the version, including eight attached to the floor.

    INTERIOR RACK

    The interior rack frees up floor space and lets you carry items up to 2m long and 13kg in weight.

    WOODEN FLOOR AND MID-HEIGHT SIDE TRIM

    Wooden floor with anti-slip surface and mid-height trim to effectively protect the cargo area.

    REAR DOOR CATCH LOCK

    All-New Trafic has a system for securing the left rear door allowing you to carry long items with the right rear door open when you need to.

    STANDARD TOWBAR

    Intended for intensive use, the standard towbar is highly versatile with a variety of hooks, ball hitch or drawbar that can be fixed to the plate.

    HANDS-FREE CARD

    When your hands are full, we make it easy for you to get into and start your vehicle by simply pressing the door or start-up buttons.
