    Towbars

    Renault goes above and beyond even the strictest European standards (CE 94/20). Thanks to further testing, Renault have also made sure that your vehicle won't be damamged, even with intensive commercial towbar use. Finally, Renault also take the most demanding climatic conditions into account by applying an anti-corrosion surface treatment.

    Aluminium Roof Rack, Walkway and Ladder

    Specially design for the New Master, the aluminium roof rack is equipped with a rear sliding roller to aid loading. The maximum load is 149kg, the galvanised steel ladder and walkway facilitate loading onto the rack.

    Ready4work

    Ready racked storage system.

    What is Ready4Work?

    Ready4Work is a racking system which is fitted before it arrives at the dealer.

    What are the benefits to you?

    • Reduction in leadtime (the vehicle arrives at the dealer Ready4Work)
    • Competitively priced
    • Covered by the same 4yr / 100k mile warranty as your new Renault LCV
    • Can be included in a finance agreement

    Kit 1

    Basic Storage System

    This kit has been designed to provide engineering tradesmen with an economical racking system suitable for the storage of a wide range of tools, components and accessories, with the option to add additional features. The system comprises three pre-assembled sections designed for simple and rapid installation whilst providing a safe and roadworthy installation.

    FLOOR PLAN

    A two part phenolic coated floor (cut 9mm plywood base with anti-slip phenolic coating to the top face). This fits directly into the vehicle without drilling, pre-fitted attachment brackets connect to the existing load lashing points.

    Kit 2

    Specialist Storage System

    System 2 is constructed from the same lightweight high strength steel materials as System 1. This system has been designed to suit the more specialised tradesman, requiring an improved system for the storage of tools and service equipment.

    TOOL STORAGE UNIT

    The rear left-hand unit and right-hand forward tool panel assembly, including x3 Euro Bins and x2 750mm Storage Baskets are identical to Kit 1. The rear right-hand unit now also includes: An additional high quality tool storage system comprising a telescopic drawer unit module consisting of x2 500mm wide x 355mm deep x 75mm high drawers and x1 500mm wide x 355mm deep x 150mm high drawer. Adjacent to the drawer unit is a 500mm wide x 355mm deep x 75mm high shelf.
