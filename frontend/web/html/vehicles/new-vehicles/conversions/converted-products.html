    Meeting your needs

    Renault now offers a comprehensive range of factory converted products that are available to order directly from our dealer network as a “One-Stop Shop”. If the factory products are not quite what you are looking for, we also offer a range of chassis and platform cabs that can be converted to meet your exact needs.

    Ambulances & Emergency services

    Renault has the perfect range of vehicles for meeting the needs of customers requiring Passenger and Patient carrying transport, which is why Renault has been the first choice for converters and customers for many years.

    Our front wheel drive vehicles offer great flexibility and with their low floor are ideal for access to and from the passenger area. Our converter partners are able to offer a great selection of vehicles to meet your specific operational and budget needs, many of them providing solutions for carrying wheelchair users.

    Both our Trafic and Master are ideally suited as passenger carrying vehicles, with many Ambulance Authorities choosing Master for transporting patients.

    Renault has been working hand in hand with its converter partners to ensure that they have all the technical information and assistance they need to meet type approval regulations.

    So whether you are looking for a straightforward Minibus or something more bespoke we have industry experts at your disposal, in the knowledge that you have the support of Europe’s No. 1 van Manufacturer.

    REFRIGERATED VANS

    Temperature control solutions.

    Converters that provide refrigerated vehicles

    • Cool Kit

    Temperature control

    Through our converter partners we are able to provide a solution for all your temperature controlled needs, from the smaller Renault Kangoo Van, to the Trafic Van and on to the numerous versions of the versatile Master Van and Chassis Cab range.

    Both Panel Van and Box Van/Chassis Cab conversions are available to your exact requirements, whether you require an insulated, heated, chilled or deep freeze solution.

    Temperature controlled vehicles can be specified to your exact market needs, where food hygiene compliance is essential. The following options can also be included: Insulated curtains for multi-drop application, Multi compartment for differing temperature zones, 240 volt stand-by, Data logging equipment.

    When required ATP certification is also available on specific conversions, for Continental transportation.

    Our converter partners will match the Renault factory warranty for their conversion (with exceptions for certain refrigeration units).

    All in the knowledge that you have the support of Europe’s No. 1 van Manufacturer.
