    MEGANE COUPE LIMITED

    LIMITED EDITION

    Expect More... Renault Limited

    With Renault Limited, enjoy a range of privileges: upgraded equipment and a distinctive exterior look at an even more attractive price. Travel in first class now!

    Magnetic styling

    In terms of looks, Mégane Limited stands out with its 16" wheels, black headlight masks and LED daytime running lights. Mégane Coupé's tinted windows and glossy black door mirrors enhance the colour of the car. A chrome-finish badge and 'LIMITED' doorframe add an elegant finishing touch.

    Packed with equipment as standard

    Leather steering wheel as standard, dual-zone climate control, rain and light sensors, electric rear windows and folding wing mirrors. Everything you need for the most comfortable drive. Parking is made easy with the rear parking sensor, and Mégane Limited boasts electronic stability control (ESC), an anti-lock braking system (ABS) with emergency brake assist, and cruise control/speed limiter.

    Power and generosity

    Designed to meet all your needs, Mégane Limited ships with high-performance petrol and diesel engines. Choose between different power levels and a manual or automatic dual-clutch 6-speed gearbox.
