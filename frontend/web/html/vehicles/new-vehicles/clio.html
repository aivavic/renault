    DISCOVER CLIO GT-Line

    MEDIA GALLERY

    Unique and distinctive design is the hallmark of the GT-Line brand. Clio GT-Line features special bumpers and 16" GT design wheels. Get ready, Clio GT-Line is getting its running shoes on.
    16’’ glossy Dark Metal GT design wheels, specific exhaust, specific front and rear bumpers, built-in DRLs at front (exclusive to Sport Range), Dark Metal GT Pack design, Exclusive exterior colour: Malta Blue.
    Exclusive Renaultsport door sills, leather steering wheel
    Lively, responsive and efficient, Clio GT-Line is equipped with new Energy engines. These engines feature innovative technologies, some of which are based on Renault Sport expertise. Also equipped with Stop & Start technology, Clio GT-Line combines sporty looks with fuel consumption and CO2 emissions that are among the lowest in its class.
