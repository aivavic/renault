    Features

    CHARACTERISTICS

    Captur has even more surprises in store.

    • Comfort
    • Technology
    • Safety

    Comfort

    HIGH DRIVING POSITION

    Enjoy the view with Captur. With its large wheels and high ride height, this crossover gives you a broad, far-reaching view of the road and everything around it. Wherever you go, you won't miss a thing!

    TWO-WAY ADJUSTMENT STEERING COLUMN

    Adjust the height and reach of your steering wheel for greater comfort. Along with the height of your seat.

    REAR COMFORT

    Enjoy all the comfort of the reclining 60:40 split-folding rear seating, which slides over 16cm.

    MODULAR LAYOUT

    Think big with Renault Captur! For maximum comfort, the interior has a modular layout. Adjust the interior space to suit, with a sliding 3-seater in the rear that folds down.

    The boot features a multi-position floor that can be removed or turned over*, with a rubber side that can be cleaned with a sponge. In the raised position, the floor conceals items that you don't want to show, while in the lower position, it provides a useful complementary loading area.

    *On Dynamique and above

    REMOVABLE SEAT COVERS

    Keeping the seats clean is easy! The zip and velcro systems let you remove the front and rear seat covers. They are guaranteed to maintain their appearance for five machine washes, and are available for purchase in store in a variety of colours. Customise your Captur with the collection of covers available to order.

    *On Dynamique and Dynamique S

    HANDS-FREE CARD

    Lock, unlock and start the Captur without even inserting the hands-free card into the reader.

    The vehicle locks/unlocks when you brush the handle or boot lid. To start, simply press the 'Start' button.

    Technology

    MULTIMEDIA FOR ALL

    Onboard Captur, choose between two innovative multimedia systems: MEDIA NAV and Renault R-Link*. Telephone hands-free and stream your music using the Bluetooth® function, USB sockets and jack. Get easy access to innovative and practical functions, including TomTom LIVE®* navigation, multimedia, telephone, vehicle information as well as the catalogue of downloadable apps from the R-Link Store.

    ACTIVE AIR SHUTTERS

    Enjoy improved aerodynamics and lower fuel consumption with active air shutters. They automatically adjust the air flow through the radiator in line with the engine's cooling needs.

    STOP & START TECHNOLOGY

    Captur makes life easier. When the vehicle is stationary, in neutral, the engine automatically cuts out and restarts when you engage a gear. This cuts emissions, fuel consumption and noise. Thanks Renault!

    ENERGY RECOVERY

    We have fitted Captur with the ESM system for recovering energy when braking or slowing down. It optimises your electricity consumption by helping to charge the battery.

    ECO MODE

    Adopt eco-driving in ECO mode. This adjusts acceleration, torque and horsepower management, climate control and heating, to ensure a smoother journey with longer accelerations. Improved fuel savings.

    DRIVING ECO2 IN R-LINK

    Improve your driving experience with the R-Link multimedia system featuring:

    • The journey summary: quantified information on your driving based on three criteria that impact fuel usage (acceleration, braking/anticipation and gear changes)
    • Advice: tips to help you do better
    • 'My preference trip': record your fuel usage on frequent routes (homework) to improve your performance

    RENAULT R-LINK - MULTIMEDIA

    With Renault R-Link, connect your smartphone to your car. With a large touch screen, modern interfaces, detailed graphics, and easily identifiable icons... Make life easier: answer calls while driving using the controls on the steering column. With the Bluetooth® function and USB sockets and jack, you can easily listen to music, or view photos and videos.

    Looking for traffic information? With TomTom® connected navigation and its LIVE services, enjoy the best in real-time information to get where you're going faster! Use less fuel driving Eco2 (journey summaries and customised tips). With the Renault R-Link store, the first in-car 'app store', download a wide range of applications via MyRenault, to make life easier.

    RENAULT R-LINK - TELEPHONE

    It's easy to make and answer calls with Renault R-Link. It synchronizes your directory and displays your contacts with their photos* (*depending on your phone).
    Dial a number or browse your contacts on the Renault R-Link touch screen... Nothing could be easier when stationary; when driving, use the voice recognition feature.

    RENAULT R-LINK - NAVIGATION

    Enjoy all the advantages of TomTom® connected navigation and its LIVE services: Use HD Traffic® to calculate your journey time, get weather forecasts, do local searches, and receive road safety warnings.

    RENAULT R-LINK - SERVICES AND VEHICLE

    Onboard Captur, you can access pre-installed applications (Email, Renault R-Link Tweet, Weather, Renault assistance) and many others from the R-Link Store. In-car applications (e-guide, fuel prices), news (Euronews, city news), practical information (TomTomplaces), fun, cultural and tourist features... you’re spoiled for choice!

    Safety

    SIDE, DRIVER AND PASSENGER AIRBAGS

    Renault Captur comes with large-volume driver and passenger airbags as well as side head and thorax airbags. A safe drive for everybody.

    CRUISE CONTROL - SPEED LIMITER

    Set your cruising speed or maximum speed using the cruise control speed limiter. Available on all equipment levels. You can then adjust the set speed using the steering column controls.

    ELECTRONIC STABILITY CONTROL (ESC)

    The electronic stability control (ESC), lets you keep your vehicle under control in the event of a temporary loss of grip. The brakes act on one or more wheels to keep the vehicle on course. Drive with complete peace of mind.

    EMERGENCY BRAKE ASSIST

    Emergency brake assist reduces stopping distances by applying maximum braking power more quickly. Standard, along with the anti-lock brake system.

    ADDITIONAL CORNERING LIGHTS

    Identify obstacles more quickly with the additional turning lights on the Captur. The lighting varies with the steering wheel angle and vehicle speed. Now you can turn in complete safety in poorly lit areas!

    CLEAR VIEW KEY

    Would you like clear, frost-free windows? No problem with Clear View Key. Designed to optimise the intensity and direction of the air flow, this system switches on the air conditioning using the electric de-icing function. Let the car take over, and just enjoy driving.

    HEATED DOOR MIRRORS

    Say farewell to condensation on your door mirror! A resistor behind the mirror heats the surface when the function is activated.

    5-STAR RATING FROM EURO NCAP

    Your safety is our priority! The proof: Renault Captur scored 5 stars in the passive safety crash tests carried out by the independent body EuroNCAP. This result follows the introduction of more stringent requirements in January 2013, once again proving Renault's expertise in passive safety. Drive safely!

    CENTRAL LOCKING REAR DOORS AND WINDOWS

    Lock rear doors and windows to prevent children from opening them while the vehicle is moving. Nothing could be easier with the switch on the driver’s door!

    HIGH GROUND CLEARANCE

    With Captur, drive safely even in difficult conditions (potholes, dirt roads, speed humps).
    Even on uneven roads or potholes, no need to worry about damaging the underbody. Ready, set, go!

    INDICATORS WITH MOTORWAY MODE

    When changing lanes, the indicator control flashes three times when pressed.

    HILL-START ASSIST

    Starting the car on steep slopes is no longer a worry. When you take your foot off the brake, hill-starting assist takes over and holds the vehicle steady for two seconds. This gives you time to accelerate normally without stalling or rolling back.

    REAR PARKING DISTANCE CONTROL

    Parking can be tricky, which is why Captur comes with a parking distance control function. This function emits an alarm to warn you of any obstacles behind the car (beeps becoming more frequent as you move closer).

    REVERSING CAMERA

    Parallel parking? Nothing could be simpler with the built-in reversing camera at the rear of the vehicle. You can see the image on the MEDIA NAV or R-Link screen.
