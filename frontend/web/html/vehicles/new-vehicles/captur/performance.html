    Performance

    PERFORMANCE

    Experience Captur's innovative engines, combining driving pleasure with fuel efficiency and reduced CO2 emissions.

    FOR MORE INFORMATION ON

    • ENERGY TCE 90 ENGINE
    • ENERGY DCI 90 ENGINE
    • TCE 120 ENGINE

    ENERGY TCE 90

    A three cylinder turbocharged 898 cc engine developing 90 hp, the new Energy Tce 90 has energy to burn. Enjoy the powerful 135 Nm of torque, available from the lowest revs (90% of torque available at 1,650 rpm). And all this with fuel efficiency of around 57 mpg and CO2 emissions of 113g/km*. Here we go!

    ENERGY DCI 90

    Enjoy a better, more fuel-efficient drive with the new Energy DCi 90 engine, simply the best of its generation! With combined cycle fuel consumption of around 63mpg and emissions of 95g CO2/km*, you enjoy a better drive and economy. With the 90hp and 200Nm torque available at all low revs, step on it!

    TCE 120

    Looking for a powerful, fuel-efficient engine? Discover the EDC Tce 120. This engine delivers a lively and economical drive with its 120hp and 190Nm of torque available from 2,000rpm. To top it all, enjoy the smoothest of drives with the EDC 6-speed automatic dual-clutch gearbox mated to the EDC Tce 120 engine.

    *Official Fuel consumption figures are produced for comparison purpose and are measured according to the European Directive 80/1268/EEC. These figures are specific for a vehicle type and not for a single car. Real world fuel (actual) consumption can vary as the fuel efficiency of a vehicle is influenced by driving behaviour, weather and road conditions. The use of technical equipment for example air conditioning, can also effect the fuel consumption. For further information, please visit the Vehicle Certification Agency web site www.vca.gov.uk.

    EDC AUTOMATIC GEARBOX

    We developed the EDC 6-speed automatic dual-clutch gearbox to deliver a highly comfortable ride. Available with the Energy TCe 120 and dCi 90 engines, it lets you accelerate smoothly without jerking or loss of power. Fuel consumption is comparable to that of a manual gearbox.

    TECHNOLOGICAL EXCELLENCE

    Captur embodies the engine expertise developed by Renault over 35 years of Formula 1 racing and 11 world championship titles:

    • Downsizing cuts fuel consumption with no trade-off in power
    • Thermo-management accelerates the ramp-up in engine temperature and thus energy efficiency
    • Stop & Start saves fuel when the vehicle is stationary and starts it again almost immediately, as part of a smooth, vibration-free process
    • During deceleration, with the ESM (Energy Smart Management) system that recovers energy when braking, the alternator charges the battery which powers the heating and air-conditioning, radio, headlights and electric power steering...

    Discover the comfort and response of the EDC automatic dual-clutch gearbox. This gearbox is available on Captur with the dCi 90 and TCe 120 engines. You will notice the difference: both fuel consumption and CO2 emissions are lower, comparable to those obtained with a manual gearbox.
